# Session 1 — Rise of the Runelords

*Sandpoint, Varisia — 1st of Lamashan, 4707 AR*

---

The coastal town of **Sandpoint** sits where the Lost Coast Road bends south toward Magnimar, a modest settlement of perhaps twelve hundred souls perched on a rocky promontory above the Varisian Gulf. To outsiders it is a pleasant waypoint — fishing boats in the harbour, the smell of salt and cedar, a cathedral still being finished after the fire five years ago. To those who live here it is simply home.

Today is the **Swallowtail Festival**, the consecration day of the new Cathedral of Desna. The whole town has turned out. Vendors line the market square, children chase swallowtail butterflies released at dawn, and Mayor Deverin is expected to give a speech shortly before midday. The air carries woodsmoke, fried dough, and the sound of a fiddle somewhere near the north gate.

You are here. The reasons that brought each of you to Sandpoint this particular morning are your own — but the festival has drawn travelers from as far as Magnimar, and the locals are in generous spirits.
