# Shield

**Source:** PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 342
**School:** abjuration [force]
**Level:** alchemist 1, arcanist 1, bloodrager 1, investigator 1, magus 1, occultist 1, psychic 1, sorcerer 1, spiritualist 1, summoner 1, summoner (unchained) 1, wizard 1
**Classes:** wizard, sorcerer, arcanist, alchemist, bloodrager, investigator, magus, occultist, psychic, spiritualist, summoner
**Tags:** shield, cast shield, force shield, shield spell

## Casting

- **Casting Time:** 1 standard action
- **Components:** V, S

## Effect

- **Range:** personal
- **Target:** you
- **Duration:** 1 min./level (D)
- **Saving Throw:** none
- **Spell Resistance:** no

## Resolution

- **Action Type:** standard
- **Targeting:** self
- **Backend Handling:** self buff
- **AC Bonus:** +4 shield bonus to AC; applies against incorporeal touch attacks
- **Duration Rounds:** 10 rounds per caster level
- **Stacking:** shield bonus; does not stack with other shield bonuses
- **Special:** negates magic missile attacks directed at the caster
- **Notes:** No armor check penalty and no arcane spell failure chance.

## Rules Text

Creates an invisible floating force shield in front of the caster. It grants a +4 shield bonus to AC, works against incorporeal touch attacks because it is force, and blocks magic missile.
