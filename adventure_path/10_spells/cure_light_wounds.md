# Cure Light Wounds

**Source:** PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 263
**School:** conjuration (healing)
**Level:** adept 1, alchemist 1, bard 1, cleric 1, druid 1, hunter 1, inquisitor 1, investigator 1, occultist 1, oracle 1, paladin 1, shaman 1, skald 1, spiritualist 1, warpriest 1, witch 1; ranger 2
**Classes:** cleric, warpriest, bard, druid, hunter, inquisitor, oracle, paladin, ranger, witch, alchemist, investigator, occultist, shaman, skald, spiritualist, adept
**Tags:** cure light wounds, cast cure light wounds, heal light wounds, cure wounds, light healing

## Casting

- **Casting Time:** 1 standard action
- **Components:** V, S

## Effect

- **Range:** touch
- **Target:** creature touched
- **Duration:** instantaneous
- **Saving Throw:** Will half (harmless); see text
- **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); see text

## Resolution

- **Action Type:** standard
- **Targeting:** touch
- **Backend Handling:** healing for living targets; damage against undead
- **Healing:** 1d8 + caster level, max +5
- **Damage:** against undead, same amount as positive-energy damage
- **Spell Resistance:** harmless yes for willing living targets; undead may apply SR
- **Saving Throw:** undead may Will save for half damage
- **Notes:** Requires target type check once undead/living tagging exists.

## Rules Text

Channels positive energy through touch. Living creatures recover hit points; undead creatures are harmed instead and may resist or save as appropriate.
