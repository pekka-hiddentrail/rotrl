# Protection from Evil

**Source:** PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 327
**School:** abjuration [good]
**Level:** adept 1, arcanist 1, bloodrager 1, cleric 1, inquisitor 1, medium 1, oracle 1, paladin 1, shaman 1, sorcerer 1, spiritualist 1, summoner 1, summoner (unchained) 1, warpriest 1, wizard 1
**Classes:** cleric, wizard, sorcerer, arcanist, bloodrager, inquisitor, medium, oracle, paladin, shaman, spiritualist, summoner, warpriest
**Tags:** protection from evil, cast protection from evil, ward from evil, evil protection

## Casting

- **Casting Time:** 1 standard action
- **Components:** V, S, M/DF

## Effect

- **Range:** touch
- **Target:** creature touched
- **Duration:** 1 min./level (D)
- **Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless)
- **Spell Resistance:** no; see text

## Resolution

- **Action Type:** standard
- **Targeting:** touch buff
- **Backend Handling:** target buff and special defensive ward
- **AC Bonus:** +2 deflection bonus to AC against attacks/effects from evil creatures
- **Save Bonus:** +2 resistance bonus on saves against effects from evil creatures
- **Duration Rounds:** 10 rounds per caster level
- **Stacking:** deflection and resistance bonuses; do not stack with same bonus types
- **Special:** suppresses qualifying mental control; blocks bodily contact by evil summoned creatures unless the ward is forced against them or the warded creature attacks them
- **Notes:** Some effects require GM adjudication because the source must be evil.

## Rules Text

Wards the touched creature against evil attackers, evil mental control, and bodily contact by evil summoned creatures. The spell grants conditional AC/save bonuses and can suppress qualifying charm/compulsion-style control for its duration.
