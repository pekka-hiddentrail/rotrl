# Shield of Faith

**Source:** PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 342
**School:** abjuration
**Level:** cleric 1, inquisitor 1, oracle 1, warpriest 1
**Classes:** cleric, inquisitor, oracle, warpriest
**Tags:** shield of faith, cast shield of faith, faith shield, deflection field

## Casting

- **Casting Time:** 1 standard action
- **Components:** V, S, M (parchment with a holy text written on it)

## Effect

- **Range:** touch
- **Target:** creature touched
- **Duration:** 1 min./level
- **Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless)
- **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless)

## Resolution

- **Action Type:** standard
- **Targeting:** touch buff
- **Backend Handling:** target buff
- **AC Bonus:** +2 deflection bonus to AC, +1 per six caster levels; max +5 at CL 18
- **Duration Rounds:** 10 rounds per caster level
- **Stacking:** deflection bonus; does not stack with other deflection bonuses
- **Spell Resistance:** harmless yes
- **Notes:** At CL 1 this is +2 deflection AC for 10 rounds.

<!-- REFERENCE -->

## Rules Text

Creates a protective magical field around the touched creature, deflecting attacks and improving AC through a scaling deflection bonus.
