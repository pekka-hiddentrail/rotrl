# Magic Missile

**Source:** PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 309
**School:** evocation [force]
**Level:** arcanist 1, bloodrager 1, magus 1, psychic 1, sorcerer 1, wizard 1
**Classes:** wizard, sorcerer, arcanist, bloodrager, magus, psychic
**Tags:** magic missile, force missile, missile spell, cast magic missile

## Casting

- **Casting Time:** 1 standard action
- **Components:** V, S

## Effect

- **Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
- **Target:** up to five creatures; no two targets more than 15 ft. apart
- **Duration:** instantaneous
- **Saving Throw:** none
- **Spell Resistance:** yes

## Resolution

- **Action Type:** standard
- **Targeting:** multi-target ranged spell
- **Backend Handling:** auto-hit damage spell; no attack roll
- **Damage:** 1d4+1 force per missile
- **Missiles:** 1 at CL 1, +1 missile every 2 caster levels beyond 1st; max 5 at CL 9
- **Spell Resistance:** yes; check per target before damage
- **Notes:** Caster chooses targets before SR checks and damage. A single missile can hit only one creature; multiple missiles may focus one target or split across valid targets.

## Rules Text

Creates darts of force that strike unerringly unless the target has total cover or total concealment. The spell damages creatures, not objects. It cannot target a specific body part.
