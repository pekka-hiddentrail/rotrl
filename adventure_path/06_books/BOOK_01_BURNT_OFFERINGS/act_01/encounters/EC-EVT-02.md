# EC-EVT-02 - Cathedral Consecration and Alarm

**Scene:** S3 - Cathedral Consecration and Alarm
**Type:** Event / Transition
**No combat. No roll required.**

## Description

Father Zantus takes the steps of the new cathedral and begins the formal consecration prayer. The crowd quiets. A thunderstone is cracked to signal the ceremony beginning - a sharp crack echoes across the square.

Before the prayer concludes, the town alarm bell rings from the north gate. Shouting follows. The consecration is immediately abandoned as the crowd turns toward the noise.

## Town State Change

NORMAL to CRISIS. This event unlocks Scene S4 (Goblin Assault).

## GM Notes

The thunderstone crack can be mistaken for the first sign of trouble - play the brief moment of ambiguity (crowd hesitates, then the bell clarifies everything). Father Zantus steps back inside the cathedral doors. He does not fight.
