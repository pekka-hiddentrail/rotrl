# EC-COM-02 — Wave 2: Fire Phase (Goblin Arsonists)

**Scene:** S5 — Escalation: Fire & Cavalry  
**System:** Pathfinder 1e  
**CR:** 2–3 (rooftop goblins + alley goblins)  
**Trigger:** Warchanter silenced / Wave 1 ends

---

## Setup

As Wave 1 collapses, goblins appear on the rooftops of two adjacent buildings and in the alley south of the square. Rooftop goblins immediately throw torches. Sheriff Hemlock arrives this round with 4 militia; he organises civilians toward the cathedral and takes crossbow shots at the rooftops. He does not compete with PC kills.

---

## Combatants

### Rooftop Goblins (3–4)
CR 1/3 | AC 20 (cover +4) | HP 5 | Init +6  
Ranged: throw torch +4 (1d2 + 1 fire, sets flammable surfaces on fire on a hit)  
**Limit:** Can throw OR move per round, not both.  
**To reach:** Climb DC 12 (building exterior), ranged attack (AC 20 with cover), or spell.  
**Morale:** Flee if 2+ rooftop goblins fall.

### Alley Goblins (2–3)
CR 1/3 | AC 16 | HP 5 | standard goblin stats  
Blocking the south exit; attack any civilian or PC who tries to leave via the alley.  
**Morale:** Flee if 2 fall.

---

## Fire Mechanics (PF1e)

Each rooftop goblin that hits a stall or wooden structure starts a fire.  
- 1d4 structures on fire at end of round 1  
- Adjacent creatures: 1d6 fire damage per round unless they move away (Reflex DC 15 half)  
- Scaffolding near the stage: collapses on round 4 if not extinguished. All creatures within 10 ft: Reflex DC 12 or 1d6 bludgeoning damage  
- Extinguishing: Full-round action at the well (30 ft west). One fire hazard per action.

---

## Wave End Condition

Rooftop goblins silenced AND alley goblins routed or killed → Wave 3 (cavalry) arrives.

---

## Loot
Standard goblin equipment. No unique items.
