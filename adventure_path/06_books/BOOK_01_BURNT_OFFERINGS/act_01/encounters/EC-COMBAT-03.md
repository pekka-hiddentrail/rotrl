# EC-COM-03 — Wave 3: Goblin Cavalry

**Scene:** S5 — Escalation: Fire & Cavalry  
**System:** Pathfinder 1e  
**CR:** 4 (3 commandos CR 1 each + 3 goblin dogs CR 1 each)  
**Trigger:** Rooftop goblins silenced / Wave 2 ends

---

## Setup

Three goblin commandos mounted on goblin dogs burst into the square from the north gate. They charge through the crowd, scattering civilians, grabbing loot from fallen stalls, and making fast passes rather than holding ground.

---

## Combatants

### Goblin Commandos (3)
CR 1 | AC 17 (base, non-raging) or AC 15 (raging) | HP 9 (base) / 11 (raging) | Init +3  
Melee: mwk horsechopper +2 (base) / +4 (raging) (1d8+1 or +2/×3, reach 10 ft)  
Ranged: shortbow +5 (1d4/×3) or +1 mounted (−4 penalty)  
Feats: Mounted Combat (negate 1 hit/round vs mount, Ride DC 10+damage)  
**Note:** Commandos started combat raging (potion already used). Stats above reflect both states.

### Goblin Dogs (3)
CR 1 | AC 13 | HP 14 | Init +3  
Melee: bite +3 (1d6+2 plus Goblin Pox)  
Goblin Pox: Fort DC 12 or 1d3 Dex damage; onset 1 day; 1/day; cure 2 consecutive saves  
Fort +5 | Ref +3 | Will +1

---

## Tactics

Commandos charge in, strike a PC or civilian, then ride away (ride-by pattern). They do not hold ground. Each round they grab one item from a fallen stall (treat as free action; mechanically irrelevant but narrate it). Goblin dogs bite first thing that comes near.

---

## Morale

**Commandos:** Flee if mount is killed or 2+ commandos fall.  
**Dogs:** Flee if rider dies.

---

## Wave End Condition

All commandos routed or killed → no active goblin threats → `%%EVENT%% attack_repelled` fires.

---

## Loot
Per commando: studded leather (25 gp), mwk horsechopper (308 gp), shortbow (30 gp), 20 arrows  
Per goblin dog: no gear; carcass worth 0 gp (diseased)
