# The White Deer
**Aliases:** white deer, the deer

## Description
A respectable three-storey inn on the north side of the central market, its whitewashed timber facade marked by a hand-painted sign showing a white stag on a green field. The common room is quieter and more subdued than the Rusty Dragon — linen tablecloths, candles rather than a fire pit, and individual tables rather than communal benches. The smell is beeswax, roasted fowl, and dried lavender hung from the ceiling beams. Private rooms are well-furnished by Sandpoint standards; two have small sitting rooms attached. A stable at the rear accommodates horses.

## Typical Occupants
Garridan Viskalai (owner and host) manages front-of-house with a deliberate formality. Two servers and a cook run the day-to-day. The clientele tends toward traveling merchants, regional officials, and wealthier locals celebrating private occasions.

## Current State
Open for business. Rooms available; rates are higher than the Rusty Dragon. Alert level: none — Garridan maintains a strict no-trouble policy and will summon the garrison at the first sign of a brawl. He is polite but cool to strangers who appear rough-edged.
