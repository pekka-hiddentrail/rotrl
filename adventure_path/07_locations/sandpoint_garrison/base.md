# Sandpoint Garrison
**Aliases:** garrison, town garrison, guard post, barracks

## Description
A compact two-storey stone building set into the north wall of Sandpoint, adjacent to the North Gate. Arrow slits flank a heavy oak door banded in iron. Inside, a common room smells of boot leather and lamp oil; a weapon rack of short swords and crossbows covers one wall. A narrow stair leads to dormitory quarters above. A small jail occupies the ground-floor rear — three cells with iron-bar doors. The building always has at least two guards on duty; a posted duty roster by the door lists patrol assignments.

## Typical Occupants
Sheriff Belor Hemlock commands twelve full-time deputies stationed here on rotating shifts. A roster of roughly forty militia can be summoned in emergencies. One or two deputies are usually found at the duty desk; others are on gate watch or patrol.

## Current State
Open to legitimate business — the public may request the Sheriff or file a complaint at the duty desk. The cells are currently unoccupied. Alert level: routine. Deputies are friendly but professional; Hemlock is approachable during daylight hours.
