# Sandpoint Cathedral
**Aliases:** cathedral, desna cathedral, chapel, temple

## Description
A sweeping structure of pale sandstone and stained glass that replaced the old garrison chapel destroyed in the Sandpoint Fire five years ago. Enormous arched windows flood the nave with coloured light during the day; at night, lanterns glow amber along the exterior colonnade. The smell of fresh sawdust still clings to some of the pews — construction was only recently completed. A wide stone plaza spreads before the main doors, used for outdoor services and public gatherings. Six smaller shrines dedicated to Abadar, Erastil, Gozreh, Sarenrae, Shelyn, and Torag line the interior walls; Desna's altar commands the apse.

## Typical Occupants
Father Abstalar Zantus (head priest) tends the building most hours of the day, assisted by two acolytes. Pilgrims and petitioners arrive steadily in the morning. During the Swallowtail Festival the plaza and nave fill with most of Sandpoint's population.

## Current State
Open to the public. No access restrictions. Alert level: none — Zantus keeps the doors unlocked from dawn to dusk. The cathedral smells of beeswax candles and recent lacquer; the pew cushions are still being installed in the side chapels.
