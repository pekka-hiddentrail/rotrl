# Sandpoint Glassworks
**Aliases:** glassworks, glass factory

## Description
A long, low building of kiln-fired brick near the waterfront, its roof vented by a row of chimneys that drift smoke even in summer. The heat from the furnaces is palpable ten paces from the entrance. Inside, the main workshop smells of charcoal, flux, and hot sand; shelves of finished glass pieces — goblets, window panes, decorative figurines — line a small showroom near the front door. The furnaces glow amber-orange; long iron blowpipes and shaping tools hang on pegs. A counting office at the rear handles orders and accounts.

## Typical Occupants
A crew of seven glassblowers and labourers works the day shift under a foreman named Gryst. Lonjiku Kaijitsu (owner) is present irregularly, preferring to manage by written orders; he is reachable most evenings at the Kaijitsu estate.

## Current State
Operational during daylight hours. The showroom is open to the public; the workshop requires escort. Alert level: none — the main risk is the open furnaces (treat as a hazard in combat). Lonjiku's recent mood has been sharper than usual; the workers keep to themselves.
