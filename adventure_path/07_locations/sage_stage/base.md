# Sage Stage
**Aliases:** sage stage, sage, stage

## Description
An open-air wooden performance platform set at the north end of the Festival Grounds, facing the cathedral plaza across a wide clearing. The stage is elevated roughly four feet above the cobblestones on heavy timber supports; three short steps lead up from either side. Bunting and coloured paper lanterns are strung above it during festival days. The stage is named informally for Ezakien Tobyn, the late "sage" priest who inaugurated it; most townsfolk have forgotten the origin. When not in use, canvas covers protect the planking. Sightlines from the stage reach across most of the festival square.

## Typical Occupants
During the Swallowtail Festival: Father Abstalar Zantus (dedicatory speech and swallowtail release), Mayor Kendra Deverin (civic address), and festival performers. On ordinary days: empty, sometimes used by children as a climbing frame.

## Current State
Currently being prepared for the Swallowtail Festival. A carpenter is finishing the railing; the bunting is half-hung. Alert level: none. The fireworks crew is due to arrive in the afternoon to set up the opening display on a platform beside the stage.
