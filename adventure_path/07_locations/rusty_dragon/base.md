# The Rusty Dragon
**Aliases:** rusty dragon, inn, tavern, ameiko's inn

## Description
The most popular inn and tavern in Sandpoint, built in a converted warehouse on the east side of the waterfront district. A carved wooden dragon, badly weathered and streaked rust-red, hangs above the door — it gives the place its name. The common room is large and deliberately mismatched: long communal tables, a fire pit rather than a hearth, and wall-hung trophies of dubious provenance. The smell is warm bread, frying garlic, and cedar smoke. A small stage in the corner sees irregular musical performances. Private rooms upstairs are modest but clean; Ameiko keeps a personal suite at the rear.

## Typical Occupants
Ameiko Kaijitsu (proprietor and head cook) is almost always present, either behind the bar or in the kitchen. Two or three servers work the room during busy hours. A regular crowd of dockworkers, merchants, and off-duty guards fills the tables from late afternoon onward.

## Current State
Open during normal business hours (late morning through midnight). All private rooms are available unless the festival draws a full house. Alert level: none — Ameiko keeps order with a long-handled ladle and a reputation for evicting troublemakers personally. Prices are fair; Ameiko extends credit to known locals and adventurers who have helped the town.
