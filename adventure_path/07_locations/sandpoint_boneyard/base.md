# Sandpoint Boneyard
**Aliases:** boneyard, cemetery, graveyard

## Description
The town cemetery occupies a walled acre behind the cathedral, separated from the main building by a narrow path of crushed gravel. Old elms and cypresses line the interior of the low stone wall; their roots have heaved some of the older grave markers at awkward angles. The smell is damp earth, lichen, and rain-wet stone. Newer plots near the east wall are well-tended with fresh gravel borders; older sections toward the back are overgrown with moss and wild grass. A locked iron gate separates the grounds from the cathedral's rear garden. A few family crypts — stone-door chambers sunk half into the ground — cluster in the northwest corner.

## Typical Occupants
Usually empty. Father Zantus visits on religious observance days. Groundskeeper Naffer Vosk (a limping, taciturn man) is present most mornings, trimming grass and righting fallen markers.

## Current State
Publicly accessible during daylight hours; the main gate is locked after dark. Alert level: none. The Tobyn family crypt in the northwest corner has been sealed since the Sandpoint Fire; its lock is old but functional.
