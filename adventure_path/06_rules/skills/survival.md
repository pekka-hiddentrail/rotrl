# Survival

**Triggers:** survival, track, tracking, follow tracks, tracks, navigate, get along in the wild, predict weather, find food, find shelter, forage, wilderness, outdoor hazard, lost, trail, which way did they go, signs of passage, hunt, follow the trail

## Resolution

**DC check using Wisdom modifier + Survival ranks.**

| DC | Task |
|---|---|
| 10 | Get along in the wild (move at half overland speed, no supplies needed). Feed 1 additional person per 2 points above DC 10. |
| 15 | Gain +2 Fortitude vs severe weather while moving at half speed, or +4 if stationary. Grant same bonus to 1 other person per 1 point above DC 15. |
| 15 | Keep from getting lost or avoid natural hazards (quicksand, etc.). |
| 15 | Predict weather up to 24 hours ahead. For every 5 points above DC 15, predict 1 additional day ahead. |

### Tracking — Ground Surface

| Surface | Base DC |
|---|---|
| Very soft (fresh snow, thick dust, wet mud) | 5 |
| Soft (loose dirt, thick grass) | 10 |
| Firm (lawns, fields, woods, dirty floors) | 15 |
| Hard (bare rock, indoor floor, streambeds) | 20 |

### Tracking — Condition Modifiers

| Condition | DC Modifier |
|---|---|
| Every three creatures in the group being tracked | −1 |
| Creature size: Fine / Diminutive / Tiny / Small | +8 / +4 / +2 / +1 |
| Creature size: Medium | +0 |
| Creature size: Large / Huge / Gargantuan / Colossal | −1 / −2 / −4 / −8 |
| Every 24 hours since the trail was made | +1 |
| Every hour of rain since the trail was made | +1 |
| Fresh snow since the trail was made | +10 |
| Poor visibility: overcast or moonless night | +6 |
| Poor visibility: moonlight | +3 |
| Poor visibility: fog or precipitation | +3 |
| Tracked party hides trail (moves at half speed) | +5 |

For size modifiers, apply only the largest size in the group. For poor visibility, apply only the largest modifier that applies.

**Tracking speed:** Half speed = no penalty. Normal speed = −5. Up to twice normal speed = −20.

**Untrained tracking:** Untrained characters can make checks to find tracks, but can only follow them if the DC is 10 or lower.

**Perception alternative:** Perception can find a footprint or sign of passage using the same surface DCs, but cannot be used to follow a trail even if someone else found it first.

## GM Notes

- **Post-raid tracking** — goblins are Small (−1 DC modifier per three goblins in the war band). Firm ground outside Sandpoint = DC 15. If the party follows within an hour or two, time modifier is minimal (+1 per 24 h is forgiving). A light coastal rain during the night adds +1 per hour of rain.
- **Nettlewood approach** — firm ground with thick undergrowth: DC 15 to stay on course toward Thistletop; failure adds 1d4 × 10 minutes of wandering through briars.
- **Coastal weather** — DC 15 Survival in the morning predicts incoming fog or storm off the Varisian Gulf; add 5 more points to forecast a second day out. Relevant for any sea-cliff scene at Thistletop.
- **Goblin camp signs** — a DC 12 check near the Nettlewood fringe reveals campfire ash, gnawed bones, and Small-sized footprints converging on a single path (Size modifier makes this easier than firm-ground baseline).

## What It Does

Survival covers outdoor lore: finding food and water, avoiding natural hazards, predicting weather, keeping a group safe on the road, and following tracks left by creatures or people.

## Untrained

Can be attempted untrained for most uses. Tracking untrained is possible for finding tracks, but you can only follow them if the tracking DC is 10 or lower. Perception can locate individual footprints using the same surface DCs but cannot follow a trail.

## Action

Varies. Most Survival checks (foraging, navigation, weather prediction) represent hours or a full day of activity. Finding or following tracks is at least a full-round action and may take longer depending on terrain.

## Try Again

Varies by task:
- **Get along in the wild, Fortitude bonus:** Once per 24 hours; the result applies until the next check.
- **Avoid getting lost / natural hazards:** When the situation calls for it; no retry for the same specific situation or hazard.
- **Find tracks:** Retry after 1 hour of searching outdoors, or 10 minutes indoors.

## Special

- A trained Survival user can automatically determine true north.
- Rangers gain a bonus on Survival checks when tracking a favored enemy.
