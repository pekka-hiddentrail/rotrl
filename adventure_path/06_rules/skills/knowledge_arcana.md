# Knowledge (Arcana)

**Triggers:** knowledge arcana, arcane knowledge, know arcana, identify spell, identify magic, identify aura, detect magic, what school, what spell, magical beast, dragon, construct, what is this magic, magical tradition, ancient mystery, arcane symbol, arcane rune, magical properties, enchanted, what kind of magic, is this magical, what does this do magically, how does this work magically

## Resolution

**DC check using INT modifier + Knowledge (Arcana) ranks.** Untrained characters (0 ranks) can attempt DC 10 checks only.

| DC | Information Type |
|---|---|
| 10 | Common arcane knowledge; major schools of magic; well-known magical creatures |
| 15 | Identify auras while using detect magic (DC 15 + spell level) |
| 20 | Identify materials manufactured by magic; identify spells cast using a specific material component |
| 20 + spell level | Identify a spell effect that is in place |
| 25 + spell level | Identify a spell that just targeted you |

## Identifying Creatures (Arcana covers constructs, dragons, magical beasts)

Base DC = **10 + CR**. Common creatures = **5 + CR**. Rare creatures = **15 + CR** or more.
For every 5 by which you exceed the DC, you recall one additional useful fact.

| Creature Type | Base DC |
|---|---|
| Construct (golem, animated object) | 10 + CR |
| Dragon (any) | 10 + CR |
| Magical beast (basilisk, manticore, etc.) | 10 + CR |
| Common magical beast (griffon, hippogriff) | 5 + CR |

## Sample Questions (Campaign)

| Question | DC |
|---|---|
| Is this object magical? (with detect magic active) | 15 + aura strength |
| What school is the magic on this item? | 15 + spell level |
| This rune is glowing — is it arcane in nature? | 15 |
| What is a wand of cure light wounds? How does it work? | 10 |
| What is a sinspawn? (construct/magical beast? — actually aberration, defer to Dungeoneering) | — |
| What kind of creature is this (magical beast)? | 5–15 + CR |

## GM Notes

- Arcana does not replace Spellcraft for actively identifying magic during casting — it covers knowledge and recall
- Thassilonian rune magic is covered by both Arcana and History; either skill unlocks partial information; History gives the political/historical context, Arcana gives the magical mechanics
- If PCs find a magic item and have detect magic active, Arcana identifies school and strength; Spellcraft (DC 15 + spell level) identifies the specific spell

## What It Does

Knowledge (Arcana) covers ancient mysteries, magic traditions, arcane symbols, constructs, dragons, and magical beasts. It is the primary skill for recalling magical lore and identifying magically created creatures or effects.

## Untrained

Can be attempted untrained for DC 10 or lower. Access to an arcane library removes the DC 10 limit but increases check time to 1d4 hours.

## Action

Usually none. Recalling knowledge is mental and instantaneous.

## Try Again

No. The check represents what you know — thinking about a topic a second time doesn't let you know something you never learned.

## Special

A bard adds half class level (minimum 1) on all Knowledge checks, even untrained.
A wizard with Arcane Bond (familiar) may gain a bonus on Arcana checks per the familiar type.
