# Knowledge (Planes)

**Triggers:** knowledge planes, know planes, planar knowledge, what plane, which plane, planar, outsider, demon, devil, angel, celestial, fiend, fiendish, infernal, abyssal, abyss, hell, heaven, identify outsider, identify demon, identify devil, identify angel, what kind of demon, what kind of devil, elemental, what is this creature, runewell, what powers this, what is this energy, what is the nature of, extraplanar, summoned, conjured

## Resolution

**DC check using INT modifier + Knowledge (Planes) ranks.** Untrained characters (0 ranks) can attempt DC 10 checks only.

| DC | Information Type |
|---|---|
| 10 | Know the names of the planes; basic alignment of major planes |
| 15 | Recognize the current plane; names and broad descriptions of major planes (Abyss, Hell, Elysium, etc.) |
| 20 | Identify a creature’s planar origin; specific plane geography or politics |
| 25 | Runewell mechanics (stores power from souls aligned to a sin); planar binding implications |
| 30 | Specific demon lord domains, unique outsider abilities, creation of a particular extraplanar structure |

## Identifying Creatures (Planes covers all outsiders)

Base DC = **10 + CR**. For every 5 by which you exceed the DC, you recall one additional useful fact (DR type, energy immunity, special attack, or summon condition).

| Creature Type | Base DC |
|---|---|
| Any outsider | 10 + CR |
| Common outsider (babau, erinyes, hound archon) | 5 + CR |
| Rare outsider (qlippoth, demodand, psychopomp) | 15 + CR |
| Demon lord or unique outsider | 15 + CR (or more) |

## GM Notes

- Runewells are primarily a History question at the surface level; Planes is needed to understand the energy mechanics
- Lamashtu is a demon lord who ascended — DC 20 to know her origin, DC 25 to know her planar domain and what she grants to followers
- The Abyss is the plane of demonic origin; Nualia's transformation is Abyssal in nature
- If a PC has no ranks in Planes, steer them toward Brodert Quink (History angle) or the Catacombs altar (Religion angle) for the same information

## What It Does

Knowledge (Planes) covers the Inner Planes, the Outer Planes, the Astral Plane, the Ethereal Plane, outsiders, and planar magic. In this campaign it is most relevant for identifying outsiders and understanding the Runewell’s energy source.

## Untrained

Can be attempted untrained for DC 10 or lower. Access to a planar library removes the DC 10 limit, but check time increases to 1d4 hours. A particularly complete library may grant a bonus on the check.

## Action

Usually no action required to recall information. Identifying a creature in combat is a free action (mental).

## Try Again

No. Once you fail a Knowledge check, you simply don't recall the information.

## Special

A bard adds half her class level (minimum 1) on all Knowledge checks, even untrained.
Sorcerers and wizards with planar-focused specialisations may gain a +2 on relevant checks at GM discretion.
