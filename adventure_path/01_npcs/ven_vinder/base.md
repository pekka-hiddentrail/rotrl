# Ven Vinder

**Aliases:** ven, vinder, general store, vinder's
**Locations:** vinder's sundries, general store, area 26, festival square

## Personality

A solid, hardworking man with an outsized protective instinct toward his daughters. Stubborn, suspicious of strangers, and prone to seeing threats where there are none — though in the case of Shayliss, he is looking at entirely the wrong threat. Respected in town as a dependable, no-nonsense businessman. Not a bad person — just a father who cannot see clearly.

## Appearance

A heavyset man in his fifties, square-jawed and direct, with the look of someone who has stocked shelves and settled disputes for thirty years and found the work suits him. He watches strangers the way a man watches weather — looking for signs of trouble before it arrives.

## Location & Availability — Act I

- **At the festival:** Present with his wife Solsta and daughters Shayliss and Katrine.
- **After the attack:** At the general store (area 26). Back to business quickly.
- **General:** At Vinder's Sundries most days.

## GM Notes

- **Initial attitude:** Neutral — professionally courteous; not warm, not hostile
- **Warms to PCs who:** Are respectful and transactional; cause no trouble with his family; demonstrate they are serious people
- **Cools toward PCs who:** Attract Shayliss's attention or appear to encourage it; seem evasive; ask questions about his daughters
- **Voice/mannerism:** Blunt and efficient; gives prices, not conversation; thaws slightly when talking about Sandpoint's rebuilding; guards up immediately if daughters are mentioned
- **Emphasise:** The misdirected vigilance — he is watching hard for the wrong things; his genuine love for his family beneath the bluntness
- **Avoid:** Making him simply a hostile obstacle; his protectiveness is sympathetic even when it's wrong

## Social Checks

- **Diplomacy (smooth over the Shayliss encounter):** DC 20 — he accepts the explanation but remains watchful
- **Bluff (explain the Shayliss encounter):** DC 18 — believes it but remains slightly suspicious
- **Intimidate (the Shayliss encounter):** He flees and calls for the sheriff — not recommended
- **Sense Motive (when he talks protectively about his daughters):** DC 14 — note the mismatch between his vigilance and its actual target — unlocks: Shayliss (see Secrets)
- **Gather Information (among Sandpoint locals):** DC 13 — locals know about Katrine and Banny — unlocks: Katrine and Banny (see Secrets)

## Secrets

- **Shayliss:** His daughter Shayliss has a habit of engineering private encounters with attractive male visitors. He is completely unaware and would be devastated to learn it. He is watching entirely the wrong person for threats to his family. — *Unlocked by: Sense Motive DC 14 when he talks protectively about his daughters — note the mismatch between his vigilance and its actual target*
- **Katrine and Banny:** His other daughter Katrine is secretly meeting Banny Harker (the mill worker) at the lumber mill at night. Ven suspects nothing. — *Unlocked by: Gather Information DC 13 among Sandpoint locals*

## State Handling

- **If Cooperative:** General store goods at a 10% discount. Useful local information source. Introduces PCs to Solsta's excellent home-cooked pies.
- **If Distrustful (Shayliss encounter ends badly):** Bars the offending PC from the store. His displeasure causes a –2 penalty on all Diplomacy and Gather Information checks in town until resolved. Complains about PCs loudly to anyone who'll listen.
- **If Killed:** Shayliss and Katrine are traumatized. The general store closes. A key supply resource is lost. Solsta blames the PCs regardless of circumstances.

## Narrative Function

A functional supply NPC whose primary mechanical role is the Shayliss encounter — a social trap with meaningful town-reputation consequences. His protectiveness, though misdirected, is sympathetically drawn. Mishandling his daughter costs the party social capital at a time when they need the town on their side.
