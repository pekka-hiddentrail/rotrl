# Turch Sterglus

**Aliases:** turch, sterglus, fishmarket, fish man
**Locations:** valdemar fishmarket, fishmarket, harbor, docks, area 34

## Personality

The loveable grumbler — always complaining about the weather, the catch, the young people, the price of salt — while packaging every customer's fish with a smile and a wink. Retired from fishing himself after a knee injury, he runs the Valdemar Fishmarket as if it were his own property, and most locals treat it that way. He has five exceptionally capable sons who do the heavy lifting. A repository of harbor gossip who doesn't realize quite how much he knows.

## Appearance

A stout, sun-browned man in his sixties with a permanent squint from years on the water and hands that look like they were carved rather than grown. He moves with a slight hitch in his right leg but never slows down enough for it to matter.

## Location & Availability — Act I

- **At the festival:** Running a fish stall. Complaining loudly about something but clearly enjoying himself.
- **General:** At the fishmarket (area 34) most days.

## GM Notes

- **Initial attitude:** Friendly — he defaults to warmth with anyone who treats him like a person
- **Warms to PCs who:** Buy something; listen to his complaints without rushing him; ask about the harbor or the catch; tip generously
- **Cools toward PCs who:** Are condescending; try to extract information without engaging; show obvious impatience
- **Voice/mannerism:** Constant background grumbling that coexists with genuine good humor; mixes complaints and compliments in the same breath; calls everyone "friend" as a reflex
- **Emphasise:** The way he gossips without knowing he's gossiping; his sons as an extension of his pride
- **Avoid:** Making the grumbling tiresome — it should always land warm

## Social Checks

- **Knowledge (local / harbor):** DC 10 — knows every fisherman, boat, and unusual cargo through Sandpoint Harbor
- **Diplomacy (tip generously):** Automatic improvement to Helpful
- **Diplomacy (after the attack, asking about unusual harbor activity):** DC 12 — he remembers the wagon and mentions it — unlocks: Suspicious cargo (see Secrets)

## Secrets

- **Suspicious cargo:** A wagon picked up crates from the docks two nights before the festival without the usual Valdemar Fishmarket paperwork. Turch complained but was told it was a private arrangement. He knows which warehouse it went to. — *Unlocked by: Diplomacy DC 12 after the attack when PCs ask about unusual harbor activity*

## State Handling

- **If Cooperative:** Harbor intelligence — knows if unusual boats or people have been seen at the docks. Discounted fish. His sons can crew a small vessel in a pinch if the party needs to travel by water.
- **If Killed:** The fishmarket loses its personality. His sons continue operations but the warmth is gone.
