# Gaven Deverin

**Aliases:** gaven, deverin, brewer, two knight, two knight brewery
**Locations:** two knight brewery, brewery, area 39, festival square

## Personality

Gaven lost his brother Wade — one of Chopper's first victims — and it shook his faith in Abadar deeply. He still runs the brewery his brother helped found, and the work gives him purpose, but the joy has gone out of it. Locals whisper that the beer hasn't tasted quite the same since Wade died, and there is something to that — Gaven brews with grief now. He is still fundamentally good and honorable, but quieter and more fragile than he once was. The paladin's certainty is gone; what remains is duty.

## Appearance

A broad-shouldered man in his forties, weathered from years of manual work, with tired eyes that carry more than the job requires. He dresses plainly and moves with the deliberate economy of someone who has learned not to expect much from a day.

## Location & Availability — Act I

- **At the festival:** Present. Serving Two Knight Brewery products at a booth. Quietly proud.
- **General:** At the brewery (area 39).

## GM Notes

- **Initial attitude:** Friendly — his paladin nature disposes him toward good people
- **Warms to PCs who:** Ask about the brewery with genuine interest; share his faith in Abadar; have dealt with grief themselves; do not offer easy comfort about Wade
- **Cools toward PCs who:** Are flippant about faith, dismiss grief as weakness, or push too hard before trust is earned
- **Voice/mannerism:** Measured and quiet; pauses before answering personal questions; uses "we" when talking about the brewery even though it is just him now
- **Emphasise:** The weight he still carries; the pride mixed with grief; the way duty fills the space where joy used to be
- **Avoid:** Making him maudlin or wallowing — he is functional, just bruised

## Social Checks

- **Diplomacy (ask about the brewery):** DC 10 — talks about it with bittersweet pride; Wade's name comes up naturally
- **Knowledge (Religion) re: Abadar:** DC 12 — common ground; he opens up about his crisis of faith
- **Sense Motive:** DC 14 — his faith is genuinely shaken, not just mildly troubled
- **Diplomacy (faith crisis, after Sense Motive):** DC 16 — he shares more about Wade's final days — unlocks: Wade's last words (see Secrets)

## Secrets

- **Wade's last words:** Wade told Gaven the morning he died that he had a bad feeling about a particular part of town — something he couldn't name. Gaven has never shared this because he knows it sounds like grief talking. — *Unlocked by: Diplomacy DC 16 after sharing in his faith crisis*

## State Handling

- **If Cooperative:** Access to Two Knight Brewery products at cost. His paladin abilities — detect evil and lay on hands — are available if PCs need them in a genuine crisis. He will not deny help to good people in need.
- **If Killed:** The brewery closes. One of Sandpoint's last connections to its founding-era institutions is gone. Mayor Deverin (a distant relation) mourns him personally.

## Narrative Function

Carries the human cost of the Late Unpleasantness into the present day. His crisis of faith is an honest portrait of how Sandpoint is still healing. His paladin abilities are a genuine utility resource for PCs who earn his trust and find themselves without other healing options.
