# Larz Rovanky

**Aliases:** larz, rovanky, tanner
**Locations:** tannery, south sandpoint, area 14

## Personality

A workaholic of almost legendary proportions in Sandpoint — Mayor Deverin's festival speech jokes about him attending at all, because he almost never stops working. He demands perfection of himself and his workers, dislikes small talk, and considers pleasantries a waste of time he could spend on the craft. He can shift from Indifferent to Friendly remarkably fast the moment a PC demonstrates real skill; competence is the only currency he accepts.

## Appearance

A lean, leathery man permanently stained at the hands and forearms from the trade, who smells faintly of the tannery no matter where he goes. His expression defaults to focused; he looks at people the same way he looks at a hide — assessing what it needs and how long it will take.

## Location & Availability — Act I

- **At the festival:** Present but visibly uncomfortable. Standing near the edge of the crowd, already thinking about work.
- **After the attack:** Back at the tannery (area 14) within the hour.
- **General:** At the tannery almost every day, early mornings until late at night.

## GM Notes

- **Initial attitude:** Indifferent — not hostile, simply preoccupied; PCs are an interruption until proven otherwise
- **Warms to PCs who:** Demonstrate a relevant craft skill; treat him as a professional; get to the point quickly; ask intelligent questions about leatherwork
- **Cools toward PCs who:** Waste his time; engage in small talk; show up without a clear purpose
- **Voice/mannerism:** Short sentences; no pleasantries; gives direct answers and expects the same; will turn back to his work mid-conversation if the PC stops being interesting
- **Emphasise:** Respect for competence; discomfort at the festival; pride in his craft over his reputation
- **Avoid:** Making him rude — he is not rude, just efficient

## Social Checks

- **Diplomacy (earn his respect):** DC 14 — share a relevant skill or professional credential; he opens up
- **Craft (leatherwork):** DC 10 — immediate engagement; genuine enthusiasm for discussing his trade
- **Sense Motive:** DC 12 — quietly proud to have been mentioned in the Mayor's speech, though he'd never admit it
- **Diplomacy (after the goblin attack, asking about unusual activity):** DC 14 — he remembers the wagon and mentions it — unlocks: Tannery district rumour (see Secrets)

## Secrets

- **Tannery district rumour:** One of his workers saw an unfamiliar figure entering the old tunnel beneath the tannery district late at night, three days before the festival. Larz dismissed it as a drunk taking shelter. He hasn't thought about it since. — *Unlocked by: Diplomacy DC 14 after the goblin attack, when PCs are asking about unusual activity*

## State Handling

- **If Cooperative:** Quality leather goods at cost price. Can supply material components for magic items involving leather. A useful ongoing crafting contact.
- **If Distrustful:** PCs waste his time or disrupt his workers. Refuses service. Complains to Mayor Deverin.
- **If Killed:** Sandpoint's leather supply is disrupted. Small economic consequence but a loss of a reliable craftsman and a town institution.

## Narrative Function

Color NPC who grounds the festival's sense of a real working town. His fame for working through everything is a social shorthand for Sandpoint's productive character. Useful crafting contact for the party if they treat him as a professional.
