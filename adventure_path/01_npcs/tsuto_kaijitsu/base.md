# Tsuto Kaijitsu

**Aliases:** tsuto, tsuto kaijitsu
**Locations:** glassworks, sandpoint glassworks

## Personality

Bitter, convinced, and quietly fanatical. Tsuto was never accepted by Lonjiku and grew up with that wound defining him, eventually finding purpose in Nualia's cause — not out of ideology, but because she saw him when no one else did. He is dangerous because he is committed, because he knows Sandpoint from the inside, and because the only thing that might reach him is Ameiko.

## Appearance

A lean, sharp-featured young man with his mother's Tian coloring and something harder in the eyes than his age should account for. He holds himself with the controlled tension of someone who has spent years being looked through.

## Location & Availability — Act I

**NOT AVAILABLE IN ACT I.** Tsuto is off-screen, preparing sabotage in coordination with Nualia. He does not encounter the PCs during the festival. He surfaces in Act II, at the Glassworks.

Do not reference him by name unless PCs specifically ask Ameiko about her family. Even then, she will be guarded.

## GM Notes

- **Initial attitude:** Hostile — he has no goodwill toward outsiders and views the PCs as obstacles at best
- **Warms if:** Convinced that Ameiko's safety genuinely depends on his cooperation; extreme duress breaks through the ideological shell
- **Cools toward PCs who:** Invoke Lonjiku; appeal to Sandpoint's good; try to separate him from Nualia with ideology rather than personal stakes
- **Voice/mannerism:** Controlled and cold; gives nothing voluntarily; his only visible crack is anything related to Ameiko
- **Emphasise:** The wound beneath the conviction; the way Nualia gave him a purpose that replaced belonging
- **Avoid:** Making him theatrical or villain-monologuing; his danger is quiet and committed

## Social Checks

- **Intimidate (under duress):** DC 18 — he discloses the full scope of Nualia's plan — unlocks: Nualia's plan (see Secrets)
- **Diplomacy (invoking Ameiko's safety):** DC 20 — same disclosure as above — unlocks: Nualia's plan (see Secrets)
- **Intimidate (confrontation with evidence of Lonjiku's death):** DC 16 — he admits what he did — unlocks: Lonjiku's fate (see Secrets)

## Secrets

- **Nualia's plan:** He knows the full scope of the planned second attack — the target buildings, the timing, and the goblin tribes involved. He will not share this willingly but would under extreme duress or if convinced Ameiko's life depends on it. — *Unlocked by: Intimidate DC 18 or Diplomacy DC 20 invoking Ameiko's safety*
- **Lonjiku's fate:** He killed his father at the Glassworks. He does not regret it but he is not at peace with it either. — *Unlocked by: Intimidate DC 16 or confrontation with evidence*

## State Handling

- **If Captured Alive:** Provides intelligence about Nualia, Thistletop, and the goblin coordination; creates moral tension regarding Ameiko
- **If Killed:** The truth about the Glassworks still surfaces through investigation; Ameiko grieves privately

## Narrative Function

Shows that the betrayal of Sandpoint came from within familiar structures, not only from outside. His connection to Ameiko gives his arc emotional weight — she is the one person he might have chosen differently, and she is the one he cannot protect from what he has become.
