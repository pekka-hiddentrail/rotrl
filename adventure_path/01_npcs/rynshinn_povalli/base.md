# Rynshinn Povalli

**Aliases:** rynshinn, povalli, tailor, clothing, vernah's
**Locations:** vernah's fine clothing, clothing shop, area 30, market, festival square

## Personality

Quietly melancholy beneath a composed professional exterior — she never knew her elven father and lost her mother to Chopper five years ago, channelling grief into work and building the tailoring business into something impressive. She is widely considered one of the most beautiful people in Sandpoint, a fact she finds mostly inconvenient given how many young men pay attention to her appearance rather than her craft. She does not invite pity or personal questions from strangers, but is not cold — simply professional until given reason to be otherwise, and privately holding out hope that her father is still alive somewhere.

## Appearance

Half-elf features — evidently beautiful, though her expression is composed rather than warm by default. Dress is precise and professional; her work advertises itself.

## Location & Availability — Act I

- **At the festival:** Running a clothing booth. Polite and composed.
- **General:** At Vernah's Fine Clothing (area 30) most days.

## GM Notes

- **Initial attitude:** Neutral — polite professional exterior; not cold, but closed until given reason otherwise
- **Warms to PCs who:** Treat her as a business professional; engage with her craft rather than her appearance; listen without offering easy comfort; show patience
- **Cools toward PCs who:** Are romantically aggressive; condescend; ask personal questions too early; comment on her appearance in a way that ignores her as a person
- **Voice/mannerism:** Measured and precise; speaks about her work with quiet pride; deflects personal questions with a professional pivot; pauses before answering anything that touches grief
- **Emphasise:** Competence and composure over sadness; the inconvenience of beauty; the private longing she keeps controlled
- **Avoid:** Making her a romance prop; leaning into the grief so heavily it overwhelms the professional exterior

## Social Checks

- **Diplomacy (professional engagement):** DC 12 — opens up about her business and her community of guild crafters
- **Knowledge (local) re: her father:** DC 14 — recalling her father's story and mentioning it respectfully (not romantically) earns trust
- **Sense Motive:** DC 16 — privately lonely and grieving, though she manages it with grace
- **Diplomacy (full trust established):** DC 16 — she shares her father's name and what little she knows — unlocks: Father's identity (see Secrets)

## Secrets

- **Father's identity:** She knows her father's name — Alastir Vond, a traveling elven merchant — but not where he is or whether he is alive. She has never searched for him because she is afraid of what she might find. — *Unlocked by: Diplomacy DC 16 after she fully trusts the PCs*

## State Handling

- **If Cooperative:** Quality clothing at fair prices. Her guild network of crafters and tailors provides access to fine goods and discreet information. Genuine emotional warmth toward PCs who treat her well; good at listening.
- **If Distrustful:** PCs are romantically aggressive or condescending. Polite refusal. Not hostile, simply closed — no further engagement.

## Narrative Function

Embodies Sandpoint's lingering wounds from the Late Unpleasantness. Her half-elf nature and absent father hint at a story beyond Sandpoint's borders. Her guild network is a subtle resource for PCs who engage with her professionally rather than romantically.
