# Kendra Deverin

**Aliases:** mayor, deverin, kendra, mayor deverin
**Locations:** town hall, cathedral steps, festival square, festival grounds, festival, square

## Personality

Pragmatic, composed, and civic-minded. Balances public optimism with quiet concern for Sandpoint's security. Diplomatic by nature but firm when pressed. She does not bluster — when she draws a line, she means it. Genuinely cares about the town and its people, not just her position.

## Location & Availability — Act I

- **Festival morning:** Festival grounds, near the cathedral steps for the opening ceremony
- **During goblin attack:** Coordinating civilians near the stage; will accept PC help
- **After the attack:** Town hall, running crisis response
- **General routine:** Administrative duties in town hall; approachable during office hours

## Reaction to PCs

Diplomatic and measured on first meeting. Becomes warmer and more forthcoming if PCs defend Sandpoint visibly. Grows formal and bureaucratic if she senses recklessness or disregard for civilian safety. Will not be charmed or pressured into bypassing due process.

## Social Checks

- **Diplomacy:** DC 12 to secure official cooperation; DC 16 for sensitive resources
- **Bluff:** DC 18 — she has seen every political angle; hard to deceive
- **Intimidate:** DC 20 — will not be bullied; backlash likely if attempted

## State Handling

- **If Cooperative:** PCs gain official backing, faster militia response, access to town resources
- **If Distrustful:** Bureaucratic delays; public support becomes conditional on results
- **If Killed:** Leadership fragments into a weak interim council; civic trust is much harder to rebuild in later books

## Narrative Function

Represents legitimate authority and civic continuity. Serves as the town's official face toward the PCs. Her cooperation or distrust determines whether the party has the town's resources behind them or must work around bureaucratic friction.
