# Weapon Finesse

**Type:** General Feat  
**Source:** Pathfinder Core Rulebook, p.136  
**Prerequisites:** None

---

## Description

> You are trained in using your agility in melee combat, as opposed to brute
> strength.
> With a light weapon, rapier, whip, or spiked chain made for a creature of your
> size category, you may use your Dexterity modifier instead of your Strength
> modifier on attack rolls. If you carry a shield, its armor check penalty
> applies to your attack rolls.

---

## Mechanical Advantage

- **Substitute DEX for STR on melee attack rolls** with any light weapon, rapier, whip, or spiked chain sized for you.
- **Damage rolls are unaffected** — damage still uses STR (or the relevant ability for special weapons). Only the *attack roll* switches to DEX.
- **Shield ACP penalty applies to attack rolls** if a shield is carried
- **Unarmed strikes are light weapons** — Weapon Finesse applies to unarmed attacks, allowing her to use her DEX 2 instead of STR +0 on all unarmed attack rolls.
