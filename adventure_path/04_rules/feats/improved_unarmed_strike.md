# Improved Unarmed Strike

**Type:** General Feat  
**Source:** Pathfinder Core Rulebook, p.128
**Prerequisites:** None

---

## Description

> You are skilled at fighting while unarmed.
> You are considered to be armed even when unarmed — you do not provoke attacks
> of opportunity when you attack foes while unarmed. Your unarmed strikes can
> deal lethal or nonlethal damage, at your choice.

---

## Mechanical Advantage

- **No AoO when attacking unarmed.** Normally, attacking an armed opponent while unarmed provokes an attack of opportunity. This feat removes that provocation entirely.
- **Choice of damage type per attack.** Each unarmed strike can independently deal lethal or nonlethal damage. No penalty applies to either choice (unlike choosing nonlethal with a lethal weapon, which normally imposes –4 to attack).
- **You are treated as "armed" for all purposes** — including retaliating against a grapple attempt, threatening squares, and qualifying for feats or abilities that require an armed character.
- **Does not increase unarmed damage.** Damage die is still determined by class or size (Sacred Fist: 1d6 at level 1).
