# Magical Tail

**Type:** General Feat (Kitsune Racial Feat)  
**Source:** Advanced Race Guide, p.193 
**Prerequisites:** Kitsune

---

## Description

> You grow an extra tail that represents your growing magical powers. You gain a
> new spell-like ability, each usable twice per day, from the following list, in
> order:
>
> Disguise Self, Charm Person, Misdirection, Invisibility, Suggestion,
> Displacement, Confusion, Dominate Person
>
> For example, the first time you select this feat, you gain *disguise self*
> 2/day; the second time you select this feat, you gain *charm person* 2/day.
> Your caster level for these spells is equal to your Hit Dice. The DCs for
> these abilities are Charisma-based.
>
> **Special:** You may select this feat up to eight times. Each time you take it,
> you gain an additional ability as described above.

---

## Mechanical Advantage

- **Grants a new SLA per selection.** Each time the feat is taken it unlocks the next ability in the fixed list (Disguise Self → Charm Person → Misdirection → Invisibility → Suggestion → Displacement → Confusion → Dominate Person).
- **2/day per spell.** Each unlocked SLA recharges daily; the count does not stack if the same entry is taken twice (you always take the *next* entry).
- **Caster level = total Hit Dice.** At level 1 that is CL 1; it scales naturally with character level with no further feat investment.
- **DC is Charisma-based.** DC = 10 + spell level + Charisma modifier.
- **Cosmetic side effect:** Each selection represents growing one
  additional fox tail (up to the maximum of 8 for all selections).
