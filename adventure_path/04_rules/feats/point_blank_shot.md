# Point-Blank Shot

**Type:** General Feat  
**Source:** Pathfinder Core Rulebook, p.131  
**Characters who have it:** *(update when assigned to a character)*  
**Prerequisites:** None

---

## Description

> You are especially accurate when making ranged attacks against close targets.
> You get a +1 bonus on attack and damage rolls with ranged weapons at ranges of
> up to 30 feet.

---

## Mechanical Advantage

- **+1 to attack rolls** with any ranged weapon (bow, crossbow, firearm, thrown, sling, etc.) when the target is within 30 feet.
- **+1 to damage rolls** with the same attacks at the same range — this is unusual; most attack bonuses do not also boost damage.
- **Applies to all ranged weapons** without restriction on weapon type.
- **Range threshold is hard 30 ft.** The bonus cuts off completely at 31 ft; it does not scale or diminish.
- **Stacks with all other bonuses** (enhancement, Deadly Aim, Weapon Focus, etc.) because it is an untyped bonus.
- **Prerequisite for many ranged feat chains.** Required for Precise Shot, Rapid Shot, Manyshot, Clustered Shots, and most other combat feats in the ranged-attack tree.

### Common Feat Chains

| Next Feat | Adds |
|---|---|
| Precise Shot | No penalty for shooting into melee |
| Rapid Shot | Extra ranged attack per full-attack at –2 to all |
| Deadly Aim | Trade attack bonus for increased damage (+2 dmg per –1 atk) |
| Manyshot | Fire two arrows with first attack of a full-attack |
| Clustered Shots | Total damage before DR on full-attacks against same target |
