# Planar Savant

**Type:** Faith Trait  
**Source:** Ultimate Campaign, p.63  
**Prerequisites:** None

---

## Description

> You have always had an innate sense of the workings of the planes and their
> denizens. You may use your Charisma modifier when making Knowledge (planes)
> checks instead of your Intelligence modifier.

---

## Mechanical Advantage

- **Substitutes CHA for INT on all Knowledge (planes) checks.** This is a full
  replacement — the higher of CHA or INT is not used; CHA *always* replaces INT
  when this trait is active.
- **Applies to trained and untrained checks alike.** Any time a Knowledge
  (planes) roll is called for, CHA mod is used.
- **Does not make Knowledge (planes) a class skill.** The trait only changes the
  governing ability. Whether the skill is trained or untrained remains as normal
  for the character's class.
