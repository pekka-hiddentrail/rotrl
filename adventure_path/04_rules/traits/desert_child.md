# Desert Child (Desert)

**Type:** Regional Trait  
**Source:** Advanced Player's Guide, p.331  
**Prerequisites:** None

---

## Description

> You were born and raised in rocky deserts. You are accustomed to high
> temperatures, and gain a +4 trait bonus on any saving throws made to resist
> the effects of being in hot conditions and a +1 trait bonus on all saving
> throws against fire effects.

---

## Mechanical Advantage

Two separate passive bonuses, both trait-typed:

| Situation | Bonus | Type |
|---|---|---|
| Saves against hot environment conditions | +4 | trait |
| Saves against fire effects (spells, breath weapons, hazards) | +1 | trait |

- **Hot conditions** are defined in the Environment rules (Core Rulebook p.444).
  Temperatures above 90°F require a Fortitude save (DC 15 + 1/previous check)
  every hour or suffer non-lethal damage and fatigue. The +4 bonus makes this
  effectively automatic for Ani in moderate desert heat.
- **Fire effects** covers any spell, ability, or hazard with the [Fire] descriptor
  — Burning Hands, Fireball, a dragon's fire breath, alchemist's fire, etc.
- Trait bonuses do not stack with other trait bonuses to the same save, but stack
  with all other bonus types.
