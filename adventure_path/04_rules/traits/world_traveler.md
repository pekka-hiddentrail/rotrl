# World Traveler (Diplomacy)

**Type:** Social Trait  
**Source:** Advanced Player's Guide, p.332  
**Prerequisites:** None

---

## Description

> Your family has taken the love of travel to an extreme, roaming the world
> extensively. You've seen dozens of cultures and have learned to appreciate the
> diversity of what the world has to offer. You gain a +1 trait bonus on
> Diplomacy, and it is always a class skill for you.

---

## Mechanical Advantage

- **+1 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks.** Trait bonuses stack with enhancement, competence, circumstance, and luck bonuses, but not with other trait bonuses to Diplomacy.
- **Diplomacy becomes a class skill**, granting a +3 class skill bonus when at least 1 rank is invested. This applies even if Diplomacy is not normally on the character's class skill list.
- **Available variants:** World Traveler can apply to Diplomacy, Knowledge (local), Perception, or Sense Motive (chosen at character creation).
