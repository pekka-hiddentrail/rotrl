# Rich Parents

**Type:** Social Trait  
**Source:** Advanced Player's Guide, p.330   
**Prerequisites:** None

---

## Description

> You were born into a rich family, perhaps even the nobility, and even though
> you may have since fallen from grace or left your wealth behind, you had
> access to a finer lifestyle growing up. Your starting cash increases to 900 gp.

---

## Mechanical Advantage

- **Starting wealth is 900 gp** instead of the standard roll for the character's
  class (e.g. Barbarian: 3d6 × 10 gp average ~105 gp; Sorcerer: 2d6 × 10 gp
  average ~70 gp). This is a one-time character-creation benefit only.
- **No ongoing mechanical effect.** After character creation the gold is spent
  like any other starting wealth. The trait has no in-play bonus beyond the
  initial funding.
- **Enables better starting equipment.** 900 gp is roughly the wealth-by-level
  budget for a 2nd-level character, giving a 1st-level PC access to gear they
  would not normally afford — masterwork weapons, better armor, scrolls, wands,
  or alchemical items.
