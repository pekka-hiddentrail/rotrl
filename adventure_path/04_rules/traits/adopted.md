# Adopted

**Type:** Social Trait  
**Source:** Advanced Player's Guide, p.329  
**Prerequisites:** None

---

## Description

> You were adopted and raised by someone not of your actual race, and raised in a
> society not your own. You may immediately select a race trait from your
> adoptive parent's race.

---

## Mechanical Advantage

- **Grants one bonus race trait** from the adoptive parent's racial trait list. This race trait is selected at character creation and does not cost a second trait slot — the Adopted trait itself is the one trait slot spent.
- **No other mechanical effect on its own.** The benefit is entirely determined by the race trait selected through it.
- **Does not change the character's actual race** for any other mechanical purpose (racial HD, racial bonuses, feat prerequisites, etc.).
