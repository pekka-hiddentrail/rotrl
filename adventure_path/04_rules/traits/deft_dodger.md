# Deft Dodger

**Type:** Combat Trait  
**Source:** Advanced Player's Guide, p.329  
**Prerequisites:** None

---

## Description

> Growing up in a rough neighborhood or a dangerous environment has honed your
> senses. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Reflex saves.

---

## Mechanical Advantage

- **+1 trait bonus on all Reflex saves.** Applies to every Reflex save without
  exception — area spells (Fireball, Lightning Bolt), trap bursts, forced
  movement effects, etc.
- **Trait bonuses do not stack with other trait bonuses** to the same save, but
  stack with all other bonus types (luck, resistance, dodge, etc.).
- **Passive, always on.** No action, resource, or condition required.
