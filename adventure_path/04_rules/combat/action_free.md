# Free Action

**Triggers:** free action, drop an item, drop prone, speak, cease concentration, stop concentrating

## Rules

Free actions consume no time. You may perform any number of free actions on your turn (GM may impose reasonable limits). Free actions **rarely provoke AoO**.

Speaking is a free action even when it isn't your turn — but no more than a few sentences.

## Common Free Actions

**Cease concentration on a spell:** stop maintaining a concentration spell.

**Drop an item:** drop something into your space or an adjacent square.

**Drop prone:** fall prone voluntarily in your space.

**Speak:** a short statement (more than a few sentences exceeds a free action).

<!-- REFERENCE -->

Free actions are distinct from "not an action" — activities that are an inherent part of another action (e.g. nocking an arrow as part of attacking with a bow) are not even free actions; they simply happen as part of the main action.

GM adjudication: chaining free actions to gain a mechanical advantage is subject to GM approval.
