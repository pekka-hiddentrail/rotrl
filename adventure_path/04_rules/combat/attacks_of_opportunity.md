# Attacks of Opportunity

**Triggers:** attack of opportunity, aoo, opportunity attack, provoke, provoking, threatened, threatens, threatening, reach, reach weapon, step away, move through, casting in combat, casting defensively

## When AoOs Trigger

A creature provokes an AoO when it:
- **Moves through** a threatened square (not just out of one — moving into an unthreatened square never provokes)
- **Casts a spell** (unless casting defensively: Concentration DC 15 + spell level)
- **Uses a ranged weapon** in a threatened square (including retrieve stored item)
- **Stands up from prone** in a threatened square
- **Makes an unarmed strike** without the Improved Unarmed Strike feat (if not a monk etc.)
- **Performs a combat maneuver** without the corresponding Improved feat

## When AoOs Do NOT Trigger

- 5-foot step
- Withdraw action (for first square only)
- Total defense
- Any action that doesn't provoke (attacking, swift actions, free actions)

## Resolving an AoO

The threatening creature makes a single melee attack at their full BAB — **not** an iterative attack. This is resolved immediately, before the triggering action completes (exception: some maneuvers).

## Threatened Squares

A creature threatens all squares within its natural reach (5 ft. for Medium/Small; 10 ft. with a reach weapon; 10 ft. natural for Large). Flat-footed creatures do not threaten squares.

## Multiple AoOs

A creature can make only one AoO per round by default. Combat Reflexes feat: additional AoOs per round equal to DEX mod (minimum 1), and can make AoOs while flat-footed.

## Reach Weapons and Threatened Squares

A reach weapon (glaive, longspear, etc.) threatens 10 ft. but NOT 5 ft. A creature with a reach weapon who is adjacent to an enemy can still be attacked normally, and the reach-weapon wielder cannot make AoOs against adjacent foes.
