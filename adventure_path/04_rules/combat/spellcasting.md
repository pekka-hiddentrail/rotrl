# Spellcasting in Combat

**Triggers:** cast a spell, casting a spell, casting time, casting defensively, cast defensively, defensive casting, concentration, concentration check, touch spell, touch attack, ranged touch, hold the charge, holding the charge, dismiss a spell, dismissing, verbal component, somatic component, spell component, material component

## Casting Time

**Standard action:** spell takes effect immediately on your turn. You may move before or after casting (not both unless the spell replaces your move action).

**Full-round action:** begin casting on your turn; spell takes effect at the **start of your next turn**. You may take a 5-foot step. If you take damage before it resolves, make a Concentration check (DC 10 + damage + spell level) or lose the spell.

**Swift action:** does not provoke attacks of opportunity.

**Longer (1 min / 10 min):** cannot be cast in combat unless you have no distractions; any hit forces a Concentration check or the spell is lost.

## Attacks of Opportunity

Casting a spell **provokes AoO** from any enemy that threatens you.

**Casting defensively:** declare before casting. Make a Concentration check (DC 15 + double spell level). On success: no AoO. On failure: spell still cast but AoO still provoked — the hit may force a second Concentration check.

**Swift-action spells:** never provoke AoO.

## Concentration (Taking Damage While Casting)

DC = 10 + damage taken + spell level. Failure loses the spell (expends it if prepared; counts against daily limit if spontaneous).

**Maintaining a spell:** standard action, does not provoke AoO.

## Spell Components

| Component | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Verbal (V) | Must be able to speak — silenced = cannot cast |
| Somatic (S) | One free hand required — bound/grappling = cannot cast |
| Material (M) | Trivial cost: assumed present if you have a component pouch |
| Focus (F) | Item held; not consumed |
| Divine Focus (DF) | Holy symbol or equivalent |

## Touch Spells

After casting, you may touch the target as a **free action on the same turn**. You can move before casting, between casting and touching, or after touching.

**Touching an opponent:** requires a melee touch attack (no AoO — the attack itself is armed). The *casting* still provokes AoO normally.

**Touch AC:** ignores armor, shield, and natural armor bonuses. DEX mod, size, and deflection still apply.

**Holding the charge:** if you don't discharge the spell this turn, you hold it indefinitely. Any incidental touch (even unintentional) discharges it. Casting another spell dissipates the held charge.

**Unarmed strike while holding a charge:** the strike is unarmed (provokes AoO normally). If it hits, you deal unarmed damage and discharge the spell.

## Ranged Touch Spells

Resolved as part of casting — no separate attack action. **Does provoke AoO** even if cast defensively. Cannot be held to a later turn unless the spell says otherwise.

## Dismissing a Spell

Standard action. Does not provoke AoO.

## Concentration Modifier

Concentration = caster level + spellcasting ability modifier (Int for wizards; Cha for sorcerers/bards/oracles; Wis for clerics/druids/rangers).

## Common Concentration DCs

| Situation | DC |
|---|---|
| Damaged while casting | 10 + damage + spell level |
| Damaged by ongoing spell (e.g. fire aura) | 10 + half damage + spell level |
| Casting defensively | 15 + double spell level |
| Grappled or pinned | 10 + grappler's CMB + spell level |
| Distracted (e.g. vigorous motion) | 10 + spell level |
| Weather (violent wind/rain) | 5 + spell level (wind); 10 + spell level (rain) |

## Full-Round Spell Notes

The spell triggers at the **start of your next turn** — you can be disrupted during the entire intervening time. Readied actions can target you during this window. If you are stunned, paralysed, or killed before your next turn, the spell is lost.
