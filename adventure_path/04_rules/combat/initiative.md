# Initiative

**Triggers:** initiative, init, who goes first, turn order, surprise, surprised, surprise round, flat-footed, acts first, acting order, readied action, delay, hold action

## Resolution

`Initiative = d20 + DEX mod + misc`

Higher result acts first. Ties: higher DEX acts first; further ties act simultaneously or flip a coin.

## Surprise Round

When combat starts and some combatants are unaware:
- Aware combatants act in initiative order; unaware combatants are flat-footed and skip the surprise round
- Only a **standard action or move action** is allowed in the surprise round (no full-round actions)
- After the surprise round, roll initiative and begin round 1

## Flat-Footed

A combatant is flat-footed until they have acted in combat (or unless they have Uncanny Dodge).
- Loses DEX bonus to AC
- Cannot make AoOs

## Readied Actions

Declare a trigger condition and a standard action. If the trigger occurs before your next turn, you interrupt and act. Your initiative drops to just before whoever triggered you. If unused, the action is lost.

## Delay

Voluntarily act later in the round. Your new initiative becomes the count you act on. You can't delay into the next round.

## Re-Rolling Initiative

Initiative is rolled once per combat. Some abilities (Improved Initiative feat: +4) modify it. A readied or delayed action permanently shifts your place in the order.

## Acting in Initiative Order

Each round, combatants act in descending initiative order. The round ends when all combatants have acted. A new round begins at the top of the same initiative order.
