# Move Action

**Triggers:** move action, move up to, draw, sheathe, pick up, stand up, stand from prone, five-foot step, 5-foot step, step, climb, crawl, crawling, mount, dismount, shield, redirect spell, manipulate

## Rules

A move action lets you move up to your speed or perform any action that takes a comparable amount of time.

A move action can be taken in place of a standard action (downgrade; not upgradeable).

Most move actions do not require a skill check.

## 5-Foot Step

You may take one 5-foot step in a round when you perform **no other movement**. It can be taken before, during, or after your other actions.

- Never provokes AoO.
- Only one per round — cannot combine with any other movement (walking, climbing, crawling, etc.).
- **Difficult terrain or darkness:** cannot take a 5-foot step.
- **Speed 5 ft or less:** cannot take a 5-foot step (moving even 5 ft costs a move action for such creatures).
- **Movement type:** you can only 5-foot step using a movement type for which you have a listed speed.

## Move Actions

### Move (Movement)

Move up to your speed. Taking this move action means you **cannot** also take a 5-foot step this turn.

Nonstandard movement covered under this action:
- **Climb:** up to ¼ speed as a move action (normal Climb check). Up to ½ speed with a −5 penalty on the Climb check.
- **Swim:** up to ¼ speed as a move action.
- **Crawl:** move 5 ft while prone; provokes AoO from all attackers who threaten you at any point of the crawl. A crawling character is considered prone; standing up requires a separate move action and provokes AoO.

### Draw or Sheathe a Weapon

Drawing or sheathing a weapon (or weapon-like object in easy reach, such as a wand) is a move action.

- **BAB +1 or higher:** draw as a **free action** combined with a regular move.
- **Two-Weapon Fighting feat:** draw two light or one-handed weapons in the time it would normally take to draw one.
- **Stored item** (in a pack or out of easy reach): treat as "manipulate an item" (move action, may provoke).
- **Ammunition** (arrows, bolts, sling bullets, shuriken): drawing is a **free action**.

### Manipulate an Item

Moving or manipulating an item is usually a move action. Includes: retrieving or putting away a stored item, picking up an item from the ground, moving a heavy object, opening a door. Whether a specific instance provokes AoO is listed in Table 8-2.

### Stand Up from Prone

Move action. Provokes AoO.

### Mount or Dismount a Steed

Move action. **Fast mount/dismount:** free action with a DC 20 Ride check; on failure it remains a move action. You cannot attempt fast mount/dismount unless you could perform it as a move action in the current round.

### Ready or Drop a Shield

Strapping or unstrapping a shield is a move action.

- **BAB +1 or higher:** ready or drop a shield as a **free action** combined with a regular move.
- **Dropping a carried (not worn) shield:** always a free action.

### Direct or Redirect a Spell

Some spells let you redirect an ongoing effect to new targets or areas. Move action. Does not provoke AoO; does not require concentration.
