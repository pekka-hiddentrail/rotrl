# Attack Rolls

**Triggers:** attack, attacks, attack roll, to hit, hit, miss, hits, misses, swing, swings, strike, strikes, shoot, shoots, fire, fires, throws, thrown, ranged attack, melee attack, touch attack, iterative

## Resolution

Roll d20 + attack bonus vs target's AC (or touch AC for touch attacks, flat-footed AC if target is denied Dex).

**Hit** — roll damage, apply DR if any.
**Miss** — no damage. Natural 1 is always a miss; natural 20 is always a hit and a potential critical.

## Attack Bonus

`d20 + BAB + ability mod + size mod + misc`

- Melee: STR mod (or DEX with Weapon Finesse)
- Ranged: DEX mod; −2 per range increment beyond first
- Two-weapon fighting: −6 primary / −10 off-hand (−4/−8 with TWF feat)

## Iterative Attacks (Full-Attack action only)

BAB +6: second attack at BAB −5  
BAB +11: third attack at BAB −10  
BAB +16: fourth attack at BAB −15

## Critical Hits

1. Natural 20 threatens a critical (confirm with a second attack roll vs same AC)
2. Confirmed critical: multiply damage (×2 default; ×3 falchion/longbow; ×4 scythe)
3. Extra dice (sneak attack, flaming) are NOT multiplied — add once

## Flanking

Two allied melee attackers on opposite sides of a target grant each other +2 to attack rolls against that target.

## Cover and Concealment

- Soft cover (+4 AC) or hard cover (+4 AC, reflex save): requires an obstacle in the attack path.
- Concealment (20% or 50% miss chance): blur, smoke, dim light. Roll d100 before damage.

## Size Modifiers to Attack

Fine +8, Diminutive +4, Tiny +2, Small +1, Medium 0, Large −1, Huge −2, Gargantuan −4, Colossal −8.
