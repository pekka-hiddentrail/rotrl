# Acrobatics

**Triggers:** acrobatics, balance, tumble, tumbling, jump, leap, long jump, high jump, fall, falling, move through, move past, squeeze past, dodge past, vault, land safely, narrow surface, ledge, across the gap, dodge around

## Resolution

**DC check using Dexterity modifier + Acrobatics ranks. Armor check penalty applies.**

A successful check allows movement at **half speed** across narrow or treacherous surfaces. While balancing you are **flat-footed** (lose Dex bonus to AC). If you take damage while balancing, make another check at the same DC immediately or fall/be knocked prone.

### Balance — Surface Width

| Surface Width | Base DC |
|---|---|
| Greater than 3 feet | 0\* |
| 1–3 feet | 5\* |
| 7–11 inches | 10 |
| 2–6 inches | 15 |
| Less than 2 inches | 20 |

\* No check needed unless condition modifiers bring the total DC to 10 or higher.

### Move Through Threatened Squares (Tumble)

| Task | DC |
|---|---|
| Move through a threatened square (half speed, no AoO) | Opponent’s CMD |
| Move through a threatened square (full speed, no AoO) | Opponent’s CMD + 10 |
| Move through an enemy’s own space | Opponent’s CMD + 5 |

DC increases by **+2 per additional opponent** avoided in the same round. Cannot use Acrobatics to move past foes if speed is reduced by medium/heavy load or medium/heavy armor (unless an ability explicitly allows it). While **prone**: full-round action to move 5 feet, DC +5. If you attempt to move through an enemy’s space and fail: **lose the move action and provoke an AoO**.

### Jumping — Long Jump (horizontal)

| Distance | DC |
|---|---|
| 5 feet | 5 |
| 10 feet | 10 |
| 15 feet | 15 |
| Each additional 5 feet | +5 |

### Jumping — High Jump (vertical)

| Height | DC |
|---|---|
| 1 foot | 4 |
| 2 feet | 8 |
| 3 feet | 12 |
| 4 feet | 16 |
| Each additional foot | +4 |

All jump DCs **double** without at least 10 feet of running start. The check result equals the actual distance jumped (halve for a standing long jump). No jump can exceed max movement for the round. **Fail by 4 or less:** DC 20 Reflex save to grab the far edge. **Fail by 5+:** fall (or land prone for a high jump).

Speed adjustments: +4 per 10 ft of base land speed above 30 ft; −4 per 10 ft below 30 ft.

### Softening a Fall

| Task | DC |
|---|---|
| Ignore first 10 ft of a fall (still land prone if you take damage) | 15 |

### Condition Modifiers (stack; use worst value per category)

| Condition | DC Modifier |
|---|---|
| Slightly obstructed (gravel, sand) | +2 |
| Severely obstructed (cavern floor, rubble) | +5 |
| Slightly slippery (wet) | +2 |
| Severely slippery (icy) | +5 |
| Slightly sloped (<45°) | +2 |
| Severely sloped (>45°) | +5 |
| Slightly unsteady (boat in rough water) | +2 |
| Moderately unsteady (boat in a storm) | +5 |
| Severely unsteady (earthquake) | +10 |
| Move at full speed on narrow or uneven surfaces | +5\* |

\* Does not apply to jump checks.

## GM Notes

- **Goblin raid chaos** — festival square: scattered debris and fleeing crowd = slightly obstructed (+2). Moving at full speed through combat = another +5. Total: DC 7 to navigate freely; most characters clear this without a check.
- **Thistletop rope bridge** — 1–3 ft wide (DC 5 base), slightly unsteady (+2), slightly sloped approaching the far side (+2) = DC 9 normally. In combat add the full-speed +5 modifier: DC 14. Fall = 40 ft onto rocks: 4d6, then Swim check.
- **Catacombs slick floors** — ichor-slicked flagstone = slightly slippery (+2) on otherwise flat ground. No width check needed, but combat maneuvering incurs the modifier.
- **Tumbling past goblins** — goblins have CMDs around 12–14 (small, low Str/Dex). Half-speed tumble DC 12–14 is realistic for a trained character; remind players full-speed tumble is CMD +10.
- **Flat-footed while balancing** — players forget this. A rogue sniper targeting a balancing PC gains full sneak attack.

## What It Does

Acrobatics covers balance, tumbling, and jumping. It lets you traverse narrow or treacherous surfaces, avoid attacks of opportunity while moving through threatened squares, clear gaps and obstacles with leaps, and reduce damage from falls. You are flat-footed while actively balancing.

## Untrained

Can be attempted untrained. All uses are available without ranks, but armor check penalty frequently makes this impractical in medium or heavy armor.

## Action

None. An Acrobatics check is made as part of another action or as a reaction to a situation.

## Try Again

Not generally applicable — the check is made in the moment and its result is immediate. A new jump attempt requires returning to the starting point with enough movement remaining.

## Special

- With **3 or more ranks** in Acrobatics, you gain a **+3 dodge bonus to AC when fighting defensively** (instead of the usual +2), and a **+6 dodge bonus when taking the total defense action** (instead of the usual +4).
- The armor check penalty applies even if you are proficient with your armor.
