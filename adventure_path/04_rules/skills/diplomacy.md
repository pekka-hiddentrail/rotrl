# Diplomacy

**Triggers:** diplomacy, persuade, persuading, convince, convincing, negotiate, negotiating, appeal, request, make a case, talk them into, reason with, plead

## Attitude Scale

Hostile → Unfriendly → Indifferent → Friendly → Helpful

| Starting Attitude | DC to Improve One Step |
|---|---|
| Hostile | 25 + target Charisma mod |
| Unfriendly | 20 + target Charisma mod |
| Indifferent | 15 + target Charisma mod |
| Friendly | 10 + target Charisma mod |

Beat DC by 5+: one extra step of improvement (max two steps total).
Fail by 4 or less: no change. Fail by 5+: attitude drops one step.
Cannot use against creatures with Int ≤ 3 or in active combat.

## Requests

Requires the target to be at least Indifferent. Separate check from the attitude shift; base DC is the current attitude DC modified by:

| Request | DC Modifier |
|---|---:|
| Simple advice or directions | −5 |
| Simple aid | +0 |
| Reveal unimportant secret | +5 |
| Lengthy or complicated aid | +5 |
| Dangerous aid | +10 |
| Reveal important secret | +10 or more |
| Aid risking punishment | +15 or more |

Helpful creatures comply without a check unless the request is against their nature or endangers them seriously.

## GM Notes

- Diplomacy cannot override an NPC's core principles or force self-destructive acts regardless of the roll
- A natural 20 does not guarantee success on an impossible request
- Track attitude shifts between sessions — NPCs remember how they were treated
- Attitude shifts from Diplomacy last roughly 1d4 hours; circumstances can make them permanent
- Treat attitude shifts and requests as separate checks when both apply

## What It Does

Diplomacy is used to persuade others to agree with your arguments, resolve differences, gather valuable information or rumors, and negotiate conflicts using proper etiquette and manners.

## Gather Information

You can spend at least 1d4 hours canvassing local taverns, markets, and gathering places to gather information about a specific topic or individual. DC 10 for commonly known facts or rumors; DC 20 or higher for obscure or secret knowledge. Some topics may simply be unknown to common folk. Gather Information checks can be retried.

## Action

Influencing a creature's attitude: 1 minute of continuous interaction.
Making a request: 1 or more rounds depending on complexity.
Gather Information: 1d4 hours.

## Try Again

You cannot attempt to influence a given creature's attitude more than once in a 24-hour period. If a request is refused, the result does not change with additional checks, though other requests can still be made.

## Special

If you have the Persuasive feat, you gain a bonus on Diplomacy checks.
