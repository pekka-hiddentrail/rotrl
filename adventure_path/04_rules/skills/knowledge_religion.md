# Knowledge (Religion)

**Triggers:** knowledge religion, know religion, religious knowledge, what do i know about, recognise the symbol, recognize the symbol, identify the ritual, identify undead, identify the deity, holy symbol, unholy symbol, sacred, desecrated, divine, who is this god, what church, what faith, what religion, know anything about this deity, know anything about this creature, identify this creature

## Resolution

**DC check using INT modifier + Knowledge (Religion) ranks.** Untrained characters (0 ranks) can attempt DC 10 checks only.

| DC | Information Type |
|---|---|
| 10 | Recognize a common deity’s symbol or clergy; major gods of the Inner Sea, basic afterlife beliefs |
| 15 | Know common mythology and tenets; church hierarchy, core dogma, common rituals; identify common undead |
| 20 | Recognize an obscure deity’s symbol or clergy; obscure church history, interpret unusual rituals, recognize signs of apostasy |
| 25 | Heretical sects, suppressed rites, Thassilonian sin-magic connections to clerical magic |
| 30 | Runewell theology, Lamashtu's demon cult specifics, identify a unique or customised corrupted ritual |

## Identifying Creatures (Religion covers undead only)

Base DC = **10 + CR**. Common undead = **5 + CR**. Rare undead = **15 + CR**.
For every 5 by which you exceed the DC, you recall one additional useful fact (a vulnerability, special attack, or weakness).

| Creature Type | Base DC |
|---|---|
| Common undead (skeleton, zombie, ghoul) | 5 + CR |
| Uncommon undead (wight, wraith, specter) | 10 + CR |
| Rare undead (mohrg, devourer) | 15 + CR |

*Outsiders (demons, devils, angels) are covered by Knowledge (Planes), not Religion.*

## GM Notes

- Nualia's corruption is Lamashtu-flavoured — Religion checks are the primary tool to figure this out from clues
- The Desna ceremony is public knowledge (DC 10); anything about whether the rites were performed correctly is DC 15
- Failed checks: the PC simply doesn't recall; do not fabricate false creature information
- Paladins and clerics should get a +2 circumstance bonus on checks related to their own or opposed faiths

## What It Does

Knowledge (Religion) covers gods and goddesses, mythic history, ecclesiastic tradition, holy symbols, and undead. Outsiders (demons, devils, angels) are covered by Knowledge (Planes) instead.

## Untrained

Can be attempted untrained for DC 10 or lower. Access to a theological library removes the DC 10 limit, but check time increases to 1d4 hours. A particularly complete library may grant a bonus on the check.

## Action

Usually no action required to recall information. Identifying a creature in the middle of combat is a free action (mental).

## Try Again

No. Once you fail a Knowledge check, you simply don't recall the information.

## Special

A cleric, paladin, or inquisitor adds a bonus equal to half class level on checks related to their own faith.
A bard adds half class level (minimum 1) on all Knowledge checks, even untrained.
