# Knowledge (Local)

**Triggers:** knowledge local, know local, local knowledge, who is, do i know, have i heard, heard of, know about, know anything about, know of, familiar with, background on, reputation of, what do i know about, what do we know about, where is, where would i find, who runs, who owns, is there a, any rumours, any rumors, about the city

## Resolution

**DC check using INT modifier + Knowledge (Local) ranks.** Untrained characters (0 ranks) can attempt DC 10 checks only.

| DC | Information Type |
|---|---|
| 10 | Know local laws, rulers, and popular locations; common public knowledge |
| 15 | Know a common rumour or local tradition; town history, notable families, well-known NPCs |
| 20 | Know hidden organizations, rulers, and locations; specific NPC relationships, minor factions |
| 25 | Hidden relationships, private scandals, Sczarni activities |
| 30 | Tightly held secrets, deep family history, buried events |

## GM Notes

- A PC with local ties (Sandpoint native, grew up in the region) should get +2 circumstance bonus
- Bardic Knowledge stacks with Knowledge (Local)
- "Heard of" and "know well" are different thresholds — use the table, not gut instinct
- On a failure by 5+, the PC can recall a plausible but incorrect rumour — use sparingly, only when it would be interesting

## What It Does

Knowledge (Local) covers the legends, personalities, inhabitants, laws, customs, traditions, and humanoids of a specific region — in this campaign, primarily Sandpoint and the surrounding Lost Coast area.

## Untrained

Can be attempted untrained for DC 10 or lower. Access to an extensive library covering this area removes the DC 10 limit, but check time increases to 1d4 hours. A particularly complete library may grant a bonus on the check.

## Action

Usually no action required to recall information. Gathering information in a community takes 1d4+1 hours (as Diplomacy gather information).

## Try Again

No. Once you fail a Knowledge check, you simply don't recall the information. Further rolls are not permitted unless new sources of information are consulted.

## Special

A bard adds half her class level (minimum 1) on Knowledge checks, even untrained — this is Bardic Knowledge.
Rangers with Favored Enemy (humanoid) may add +2 on checks about that humanoid type.
