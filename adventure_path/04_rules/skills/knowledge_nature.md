# Knowledge (Nature)

**Triggers:** knowledge nature, nature knowledge, know nature, identify animal, identify plant, identify creature, what is this creature, what is this plant, natural hazard, natural phenomenon, weather, natural cycle, season, identify beast, identify fey, fey creature, monstrous humanoid, identify vermin, giant insect, giant spider, what is this insect, natural feature, is this natural, unnatural weather

## Resolution

**DC check using INT modifier + Knowledge (Nature) ranks.** Untrained characters (0 ranks) can attempt DC 10 checks only.

| DC | Information Type |
|---|---|
| 10 | Identify a common plant or animal |
| 15 | Identify a natural hazard (DC 15 + hazard's CR); identify unnatural weather phenomenon |
| 20 | Determine the artificial nature of a feature that appears natural |

## Identifying Creatures (Nature covers animals, fey, monstrous humanoids, plants, vermin)

Base DC = **10 + CR**. Common creatures = **5 + CR**. Rare creatures = **15 + CR** or more.
For every 5 by which you exceed the DC, you recall one additional useful fact.

| Creature Type | Base DC |
|---|---|
| Common animal | 5 + CR |
| Uncommon animal | 10 + CR |
| Fey | 10 + CR |
| Monstrous humanoid | 10 + CR |
| Vermin (giant insect, spider, etc.) | 10 + CR |

## GM Notes

- Goblin dogs are CR 1 magical beasts — actually covered by Arcana, not Nature (magical beast type). Nature covers goblin dogs' environment and behaviour in a general sense only
- Giant vermin (spiders, centipedes) in the Catacombs and Thistletop: Nature check, DC 10 + CR
- Monstrous humanoids include some dungeon inhabitants — cross-check creature type before ruling
- The Nettlewood approaching Thistletop: Nature checks for hazards (thorns, unstable ground), tracking signs of goblin activity, identifying traps of natural materials

## What It Does

Knowledge (Nature) covers animals, fey, monstrous humanoids, plants, seasons and cycles, weather, and vermin. It is the field of study for the natural world and the creatures that inhabit it.

## Untrained

Can be attempted untrained for DC 10 or lower. Access to a natural history library removes the DC 10 limit but increases check time to 1d4 hours.

## Action

Usually none. Recalling knowledge is mental and instantaneous.

## Try Again

No. The check represents what you know — thinking about a topic a second time doesn't let you know something you never learned.

## Special

A druid adds half class level on Knowledge (Nature) checks and may attempt them untrained.
A ranger adds half class level on Knowledge (Nature) checks related to their favored terrain's inhabitants.
A bard adds half class level (minimum 1) on all Knowledge checks, even untrained.
