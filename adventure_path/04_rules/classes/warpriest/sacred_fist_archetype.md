# Sacred Fist Archetype Overview

**Type:** Warpriest Archetype  
**Source:** Pathfinder Advanced Class Guide, p.98  
**Base Class:** Warpriest

---

## Description

> Sacred fists are warpriests who forgo their deity's weapon in favor of their
> own unarmed fists. They emulate monks, using their body as their sacred
> weapon and their faith as their shield.

---

## Archetype Modifications vs. Standard Warpriest

| Level | Standard Warpriest | Sacred Fist Replacement |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Focus Weapon | Improved Unarmed Strike (bonus feat) |
| 1 | Warpriest proficiencies | Loses all armor and shield proficiency; gains all weapons a monk is proficient with |
| 1 | — | AC Bonus (WIS to AC, see `ac_bonus.md`) |
| 1 | — | Flurry of Blows (see `flurry_of_blows.md`) |
| 1 | — | Unarmed Strike (monk table, see `flurry_of_blows.md`) |
| 4 | Sacred Weapon +1 damage | Dodge bonus to AC +1 (see `ac_bonus.md`) |
| 4 | — | Still Mind (bonus vs. enchantment +2, matches Monk) |
| 8 | — | Evasion |
| 12 | — | Improved Evasion |

> Sacred Fist retains: Blessings, Spells, Aura, Channel Energy (if taken), Fervor (a later ability).

---

## Weapon Proficiency Change

Sacred Fist loses armor proficiency entirely but gains all monk weapon proficiencies. Specific weapons:  
Club, crossbow (light or heavy), dagger, handaxe, javelin, kama, nunchaku, quarterstaff, sai, shortspear, short sword, shuriken, siangham, sling, spear.

A Sacred Fist is **not** proficient with martial weapons or firearms and can use them only at a –4 nonproficiency penalty.
