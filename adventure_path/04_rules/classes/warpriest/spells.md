# Warpriest Spells (Divine Spontaneous Spellcasting)

**Type:** Warpriest Class Ability  
**Source:** Pathfinder Advanced Class Guide, p.60  
**Gained at:** Level 1  
**Key Ability:** Wisdom (spell DCs, concentration, bonus spells)

---

## Description

> A warpriest casts divine spells drawn from the cleric spell list. Her
> alignment may restrict certain spells (see alignment restrictions). A
> warpriest must choose and prepare her spells in advance, but she can prepare
> and cast any spell on the cleric spell list, provided that she can cast spells
> of that level. To prepare or cast a spell, a warpriest must have a Wisdom
> score equal to at least 10 + the spell level. The Difficulty Class for a
> saving throw against a warpriest's spell is 10 + the spell level + the
> warpriest's Wisdom modifier.

---

## Mechanical Details

- **Prepared divine spells.** Unlike a sorcerer, a warpriest selects her spells each day and prepares them in advance. She can prepare any cleric spell of her level without a spellbook — her access is limited only by her alignment and deity restrictions.
- **Orisons (0-level) do not consume spell slots.** Prepared orisons are cast an unlimited number of times per day.
- **Bonus slots from Wisdom.** WIS 18 (+4) grants +1 bonus spell slot at levels 1–4, +1 at levels 5–8, etc.
- **Concentration:** CL + WIS modifier = 1 + 4 = **+5** at level 1 (matches sheet).
- **Divine spell failure:** Warpriests in armor would normally risk divine spell failure, but Sacred Fist may not wear armor at all (see `ac_bonus.md`). Not currently relevant.
- **Domain/Blessing interaction:** Warpriests do not cast domain spells. Blessings replace domains entirely.

## Example Spells Prepared (Level 1)

**Orisons (0-level, unlimited):**

| Spell | School | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Detect Poison | Divination | Detect poison in a creature, object, or 5-ft. area (60 ft., concentration) |
| Resistance | Abjuration | +1 resistance bonus on all saves for 1 minute (touch) |
| Stabilize | Conjuration | Stabilize a dying creature at –1 or fewer HP (close range) |

**Level 1 (2 slots):**

| Spell | School | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Protection from Evil | Abjuration | +2 deflection/+2 resistance vs. evil; blocks mental control |
| Shield of Faith | Abjuration | +2 deflection bonus to AC (scales with level) |

## Spell DC

**Base DC = 10 + spell level + WIS modifier**  
At level 1: DC 14 for L0, DC 15 for L1 (matches sheet DC column).

## Cleric Spell List Note

The warpriest can prepare any cleric spell of the appropriate level on any given day. The spells above reflect a typical combat loadout, not a fixed list. Swap Shield of Faith for Cure Light Wounds when the party needs healing capability.
