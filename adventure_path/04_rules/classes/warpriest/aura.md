# Aura of Law

**Type:** Warpriest Class Ability  
**Source:** Pathfinder Advanced Class Guide, p.60  
**Gained at:** Level 1

---

## Description

> A warpriest of a lawful deity projects a faint lawful aura. A warpriest of a
> chaotic deity projects a faint chaotic aura. Otherwise, a warpriest's aura
> is moderate and reflects her alignment.

---

## Mechanical Details

- **Passive, always active.** No action required. The aura is part of the warpriest's nature while she is living and conscious (and likely lingers briefly after death).
- **Detectable by Detect Law/Chaos/Good/Evil.** Spells in that family can identify the aura's strength and alignment. A lawful warpriest at level 1 has a **faint lawful** aura.
- **Aura strength scales with HD.** Faint (1–5 HD), Moderate (6–10), Strong (11–15), Overwhelming (16+). A level 1 warpriest is faint.
- **No mechanical bonus or penalty from the aura itself.** It does not improve saves, AC, or attacks. It solely identifies the warpriest as a creature with a lawful aura for the purposes of detection spells and effects that target by alignment type.
- **Irori (LN):** A Lawful Neutral warpriest projects a lawful aura. Effects that specifically target "lawful creatures" (such as Chaos Hammer) treat the warpriest as a lawful creature and deal extra damage — this is a vulnerability, not a benefit.
