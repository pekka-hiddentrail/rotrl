# AC Bonus (Sacred Fist Archetype)

**Type:** Warpriest Archetype Ability (Sacred Fist)  
**Source:** Pathfinder Advanced Class Guide, p.98  
**Gained at:** Level 1  
**Key Ability:** Wisdom

---

## Description

> A deity protects her sacred fist as long as he is unarmored and unencumbered.
> A sacred fist adds his Wisdom modifier (minimum 0) to his AC and his CMD. In
> addition, a sacred fist gains a +1 dodge bonus to AC and CMD at 4th level.
> This bonus increases by 1 for every 4 levels thereafter (to a maximum of +5
> at 20th level). These bonuses to AC apply even against touch attacks or when
> the sacred fist is flat-footed. He loses these bonuses when he is immobilized
> or helpless, when he wears any armor, when he carries a shield, or when he
> carries a medium or heavy load. This counts as the monk ability of the same
> name, and the sacred fist's warpriest level stacks with monk levels for
> determining the benefit.

---

## Mechanical Details

- **WIS to AC (untyped/monk-style):** The Sacred Fist adds WIS modifier (minimum 0) to AC and CMD at all times while unarmored, unencumbered, and not helpless/immobilized.
- **Applies to touch AC and flat-footed AC.** This is exceptional — most AC bonuses do not apply to touch or flat-footed. Both touch AC and flat-footed AC include the WIS modifier.
- **Dodge bonus at level 4+:** +1 dodge bonus per 4 levels (4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, 20th). Not yet available at level 1.
- **Conditions that remove the bonus:**
  - Wearing any armor (including bracers of armor — those ARE armor for this purpose)
  - Carrying a shield
  - Carrying a medium or heavy load (encumbrance)
  - Being immobilized or helpless (pinned, paralyzed, unconscious)
- **Stacks with monk levels.** Sacred Fist warpriest levels count as monk levels for this ability. If the character ever multiclasses into Monk, levels stack.

## Example AC Calculation (Level 1, WIS 18, DEX 15)

| Component | Value | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Base AC | 10 | — |
| DEX modifier | +2 | Reflex/dodge-type contribution |
| WIS modifier (AC Bonus) | +4 | Sacred Fist class feature |
| Dodge (class) | +0 | Unlocks at level 4 |
| Armor | +0 | Unarmored — armor would remove WIS bonus |
| Shield | +0 | No shield — shield would remove WIS bonus |
| **Total AC** | **16** | — |
| Touch AC | 16 | WIS bonus applies to touch |
| Flat-Footed AC | 14 | Loses DEX; WIS remains |

> **Sheet note:** A +1 Dodge feat on top of the above gives AC/touch AC 17/17/14, which matches a typical Sacred Fist level-1 sheet with the Dodge feat taken.

## Tactical Implications

- The Sacred Fist is effectively wearing Studded Leather equivalent AC at level 1 with no armor penalty, no arcane spell failure, and no check penalty.
- Touch AC 17 means touch spells and rays are no easier to land than a standard melee hit — notable against casters.
- Flat-footed AC 14 is lower, but still respectable for level 1.
- The warpriest must never wear armor or carry a shield — this is a hard constraint on all equipment choices.
