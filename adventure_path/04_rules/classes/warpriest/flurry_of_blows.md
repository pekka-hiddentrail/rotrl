# Flurry of Blows (Sacred Fist Archetype)

**Type:** Warpriest Archetype Ability (Sacred Fist)  
**Source:** Pathfinder Advanced Class Guide, p.98  
**Gained at:** Level 1  
**Action:** Full-Round

---

## Description

> At 1st level, a sacred fist can make a flurry of blows as a full-attack
> action. He may make one additional attack as if using the Two-Weapon Fighting
> feat. All attacks must be made with unarmed strikes. The sacred fist uses his
> warpriest level in place of his base attack bonus for these attacks. For the
> purpose of this ability, the warpriest's base attack bonus from his warpriest
> levels is used to determine the number of attacks granted in the flurry.
>
> Sacred fists with a BAB of +11 or higher also gain a third attack as part of
> the flurry.

---

## Mechanical Details

- **Extra attack:** One additional unarmed strike on a full-round attack, at the highest attack bonus. This is identical to the Two-Weapon Fighting feat structure for unarmed strikes.
- **Uses warpriest level as BAB** for flurry attack calculations. At level 1, BAB = 1 for flurry purposes (even though the character's actual BAB may differ — warpriests have 3/4 BAB progression, so at level 1 BAB = +0 normally). The flurry BAB is the level, not the class BAB.
- **Penalty at low levels:** At level 1, Flurry attacks are made at (level – penalty): the standard TWF penalty is –2/–2. The sheet shows +1/+1, which implies the archetype reduces the penalty — consistent with monk flurry which attacks at (BAB – 2)/BAB structure but at level 1 this gives –1/–1 off a level-1 BAB… actually at BAB +1 with TWF –2 = –1, not +1. The sheet shows +1/+1 which suggests the attack bonus is BAB +1 (warpriest level) +2 DEX (Weapon Finesse) – 2 (TWF penalty) = +1. Confirmed.
- **All attacks must be unarmed.** Cannot mix a weapon with a flurry attack. The character must go fist-only for the full round.
- **Does not provoke AoO** because of Improved Unarmed Strike. She is considered armed for all flurry attacks.
- **Damage die:** Unarmed strike damage for Sacred Fist matches the monk unarmed damage table (by warpriest level). At level 1: **1d6**.

## Tactical Notes

- At level 1, the Flurry trades a standard attack (+2 to hit, 1d6) for two weaker attacks (+1/+1, 1d6 each). On expected damage: 2 × (1d6 × hit%) vs. 1 × (1d6 × higher%). Against AC 12 enemies, Flurry is nearly always better (both hit ~75%). Against AC 17+ enemies, single attack may be preferable.
- Flurry is a full-round action — the character cannot Flurry and move beyond a 5-foot step. Position before committing.
- Strength Surge (blessing, swift action) applies to both Flurry attacks since it boosts the attack roll for the round.
