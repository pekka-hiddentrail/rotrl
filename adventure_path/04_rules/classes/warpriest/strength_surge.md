# Strength Surge (Strength Blessing — Minor)

**Type:** Warpriest Blessing Ability (Strength, Minor)  
**Source:** Pathfinder Advanced Class Guide, p.68  
**Gained at:** Level 1  
**Action:** Swift  
**Duration:** 1 round  
**Uses:** Draws from shared Blessings pool (see `blessings.md`)

---

## Description

> As a swift action you can focus your own strength. You gain an enhancement
> bonus equal to 1 on melee attack rolls, combat maneuver checks that rely on
> Strength, Strength-based skills, and Strength checks for 1 round.

---

## Mechanical Details

- **Bonus type: Enhancement.** Stacks with all other bonus types (trait, luck, morale, etc.) but does NOT stack with other enhancement bonuses to attack rolls (e.g., an enhancement bonus from a magic weapon). At level 1 this is typically a free +1 with no competing enhancement bonuses.
- **Bonus scales with warpriest level** (not standard for all blessings — check source):  
  At level 1 the bonus is +1. Some sources indicate the Strength minor blessing bonus equals half the warpriest level (minimum 1) — this would keep it at +1 through level 1 and be +2 at level 3, etc. Confirm against Advanced Class Guide errata.
- **Applies to:**
  - Melee attack rolls (including Flurry of Blows attacks)
  - CMB checks that use STR (Bull Rush, Grapple, Overrun, Sunder, Trip)
  - STR-based skill checks (Climb, Swim)
  - Ability checks using STR directly
- **Does NOT apply to:** Damage rolls, ranged attack rolls, or DEX-based maneuvers (e.g., Dirty Trick if adjudicated as DEX-based).
- **Duration:** 1 round from activation. Covers all attacks made that round plus any AoO before the next turn.

## Effect at Level 1

A Sacred Fist warpriest with an unarmed attack bonus of +2 (DEX-based via Weapon Finesse) gains +1 enhancement on the roll → **+3 to hit** for that round. Against AC 12 goblins this is largely academic, but vs. AC 17+ enemies the +1 matters.

> **Note:** The enhancement bonus applies to the attack roll itself, not to the STR score. When using Weapon Finesse (DEX substituting for STR on attack rolls), Strength Surge still applies since it modifies the roll directly.

## Tactical Notes

- Use before a full-attack round (or Flurry) to squeeze maximum value from the +1.
- At level 1, this is a modest bonus — conserve the pool for Powerful Healer unless a hit is borderline.
- Good value against high-AC enemies where a +1 shifts odds meaningfully.
