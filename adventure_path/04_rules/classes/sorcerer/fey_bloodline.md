# Fey Bloodline

**Type:** Sorcerer Bloodline (Nine-Tailed Heir variant)  
**Source:** Pathfinder Core Rulebook, p.76; Nine-Tailed Heir archetype: Blood of the Beast  
**Gained at:** Level 1

---

## Description

> The capricious nature of the fey runs in your family due to some intermingling
> of fey blood or magic. You are more emotional than most, prone to bouts of joy
> and rage.

---

## Bloodline Overview

The Fey bloodline transforms the sorcerer into a creature of whimsy, illusion, and compulsion. Its signature abilities lean into enchantment, stealth, and mobility. The Nine-Tailed Heir archetype grafts additional Magical Tail abilities on top of the standard progression, replacing the 3rd-level bloodline power.

---

## Bloodline Skills

Bluff, Disguise, Escape Artist, Fly, Knowledge (nature), Perception, Stealth, Survival, Swim.

These are always class skills for the sorcerer. Ranks invested in them receive the standard +3 class skill bonus.

---

## Bloodline Feats

The following feats are added to the sorcerer's bonus feat list (bloodline bonus feats are available when a sorcerer feat slot opens, without meeting prerequisites that would otherwise apply):

Dodge, Improved Initiative, Lightning Reflexes, Mobility, Point-Blank Shot, Skill Focus (Knowledge [nature]), Weapon Finesse.

---

## Full Ability Progression

| Level | Ability |
|---|---|
| 1 | Bloodline Arcana (compulsion DCs +2) |
| 1 | Laughing Touch (Sp) — 3+CHA/day |
| 3 | **[Nine-Tailed Heir]** Bonus Magical Tail feat (replaces Fleeting Glance) |
| 9 | Woodland Stride (Su) — move through natural difficult terrain at full speed |
| 15 | **[Nine-Tailed Heir]** Bonus Magical Tail feat (replaces standard 15th power) |
| 20 | Soul of the Fey (Su) — immunity to poison and DR 10/cold iron; animals will not attack unless compelled |

> Powers marked **[Nine-Tailed Heir]** are archetype substitutions. The standard Fey bloodline powers at those levels (Fleeting Glance at 3rd, etc.) are lost in exchange.
