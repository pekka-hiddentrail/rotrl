# Sorcerer Spells (Spontaneous Arcane Spellcasting)

**Type:** Sorcerer Class Ability  
**Source:** Pathfinder Core Rulebook, p.72  
**Gained at:** Level 1  
**Key Ability:** Charisma (spell DCs, bonus spells, concentration)

---

## Description

> A sorcerer casts arcane spells drawn from the sorcerer/wizard spell list.
> She can cast any spell she knows without preparing it ahead of time. To learn
> or cast a spell, a sorcerer must have a Charisma score equal to at least 10 +
> the spell level. The Difficulty Class for a saving throw against a sorcerer's
> spell is 10 + the spell level + the sorcerer's Charisma modifier.

---

## Mechanical Details

- **Spontaneous casting.** No preparation required. Any known spell can be cast any number of times per day, up to the spell slot limit for its level.
- **Spells known is fixed and small.** A sorcerer knows far fewer spells than a wizard prepares — 2 Level-1 spells known at level 1, growing slowly. Choices are permanent except for one swap allowed at each even level.
- **Bonus slots from high Charisma.** CHA 20 (+5) grants +1 bonus spell slot at levels 1–4, +1 at levels 5–8, etc. (standard ability-score bonus-spell table).
- **Concentration checks:** CHA modifier applies. Concentration bonus = CL + CHA modifier. A CHA 20 sorcerer at level 1 has a +6 concentration bonus; any additional +1 typically comes from a trait (e.g., Focused Mind) or similar.
- **Arcane spell failure:** Applies if wearing armor. Sorcerers have no armor proficiency and typically avoid armor entirely.

## Spell DC

**Base DC = 10 + spell level + CHA modifier**  
At level 1: DC 11 for L0, DC 12 for L1 (before bloodline arcana adjustments).  
Compulsion subschool spells gain +2 additional DC from Bloodline Arcana — see `bloodline_arcana.md`.
