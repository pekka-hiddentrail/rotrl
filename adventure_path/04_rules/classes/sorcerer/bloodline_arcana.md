# Fey Bloodline Arcana

**Type:** Sorcerer Bloodline Ability  
**Source:** Pathfinder Core Rulebook, p.76  
**Gained at:** Level 1  
**Archetype:** Nine-Tailed Heir (Blood of the Beast) — retains standard Fey Bloodline Arcana

---

## Description

> Whenever you cast a spell of the compulsion subschool, increase the spell's
> DC by +2.

---

## Mechanical Details

- **+2 DC on all compulsion subschool spells.** This is an untyped bonus that stacks with everything — racial DC bumps, metamagic, item bonuses, etc.
- **Compulsion subschool** includes: Charm Person, Suggestion, Dominate Person, Hold Person, Confusion, Command, Geas, and many others. Enchantment (compulsion) is the relevant tag on the spell entry.
- **Applies at any caster level.** The +2 is flat and does not scale.
- **Interacts with Kitsune racial magic.** Kitsune add +1 to DCs of enchantment spells. Compulsion is a subschool of Enchantment, so a compulsion spell cast by a Kitsune Fey sorcerer gets +1 (racial) + +2 (bloodline arcana) = +3 total DC bonus on top of the base 10 + level + CHA formula.
- **Interacts with Magical Tail SLAs.** The SLAs granted by Magical Tail (Charm Person, Suggestion, Dominate Person, etc.) are compulsion spells and gain the +3 combined DC bonus once those SLA selections are available.
