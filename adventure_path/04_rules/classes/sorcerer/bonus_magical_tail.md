# Bonus Magical Tail (Nine-Tailed Heir Archetype)

**Type:** Sorcerer Archetype Feature (Nine-Tailed Heir)  
**Source:** Blood of the Beast, p.?  
**Gained at:** Level 1 (partial; see progression below)  
**Archetype replaces:** Fey bloodline powers at levels 3, 9, 15, 20 (standard Fey bloodline power progression is altered)

---

## Description

> At 1st level, a Nine-Tailed Heir gains 1/6 of a new Magical Tail feat. Every
> additional level, she gains another 1/6 of the feat. When she has accumulated
> a full feat (at levels 1, 7, 13, 19, etc.), she gains the next Magical Tail
> feat in sequence.

> The Fey bloodline powers at 3rd level (Fleeting Glance) and 9th level
> (Laughing Touch upgrades, etc.) are replaced by additional Magical Tail
> selections at those levels.

---

## Mechanical Details

- **Fractional feat accumulation.** The archetype grants 1/6 of a Magical Tail feat per level. At every 6 levels this adds up to a full bonus Magical Tail feat (in addition to any taken with regular feat slots).
- **Does not replace Laughing Touch (level 1).** Laughing Touch is still gained at level 1 as normal.
- **Does replace Fleeting Glance (level 3).** The standard Fey bloodline 3rd-level power (Fleeting Glance — Greater Invisibility on self, CL rounds/day) is replaced by a Magical Tail bonus feat.
- **Magical Tail progression** (in order of selection):

| Selection # | Spell-Like Ability Gained | Usable |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Disguise Self | 2/day |
| 2 | Charm Person | 2/day |
| 3 | Misdirection | 2/day |
| 4 | Invisibility | 2/day |
| 5 | Suggestion | 2/day |
| 6 | Displacement | 2/day |
| 7 | Confusion | 2/day |
| 8 | Dominate Person | 2/day |

- **Caster level for SLAs** = character Hit Dice.
- **DCs are Charisma-based** (10 + spell level + CHA modifier).
- **DC bonus from Bloodline Arcana applies.** Charm Person, Suggestion, Confusion, and Dominate Person are compulsion spells — they get the Fey Bloodline Arcana +2 DC bonus. Combined with the Kitsune +1 enchantment racial bonus, these SLAs are at DC = 10 + spell level + CHA + 3.

## Progression Notes

- A Nine-Tailed Heir who takes Magical Tail as a regular feat at level 1 has Selection #1 (Disguise Self 2/day) immediately.
- Fractional accumulation from the archetype adds 1/6 per level on top of any feats taken voluntarily; the first archetype-granted bonus selection arrives at level 3 or 6 depending on the exact archetype text.

## Innate Use Tracking

Disguise Self from Magical Tail is tracked separately from any other source of Disguise Self (e.g., the Nine-Tailed Heir bloodline's other abilities). Each Magical Tail SLA has its own 2/day counter. See MAGIC-TODO for Tier S3 implementation notes.
