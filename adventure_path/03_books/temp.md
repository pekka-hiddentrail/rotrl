03_BOOKS/

This is where content lives, but not authority.

Each book:

Knows its theme

Knows what pressure it applies

Knows how failure propagates forward

Nothing here overrides campaign or world rules.

Acts and Encounters Are Deeply Nested (On Purpose)

This:

Prevents loading too much context

Allows precise injection of only relevant state

Keeps the GM from “remembering the future”

Each encounter file should stand alone within its act.
