# EC-SOC-04 - Aldern Foxglove Brief Social

**Scene:** S2 - Festival Social Phase (or S6 Aftermath)
**Type:** Social / Seed

## Description

Aldern Foxglove is a well-dressed nobleman attending the festival as a visitor. He is charming, attentive, and easy to like.

During the festival (pre-attack): Light conversation. Offers to buy the party a round. Curious about adventurers.

After the attack (if PCs acted heroically): His gratitude is genuine and a little overwhelming. He offers dinner at the Rusty Dragon tonight, and a hunting trip in the Tickwood tomorrow.

## Social Checks

- Diplomacy DC 6: He warms to you immediately.
- Sense Motive DC 18: Something under the charm feels slightly off - too eager, too attentive.
- Bluff DC 12: Redirect his attention without giving offense.

## Enforcement

Play him as pleasant background. His significance is only visible in hindsight. Do NOT make him seem threatening or important.
