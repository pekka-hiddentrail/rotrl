# EC-SOC-01 - Welcoming Speeches

**Scene:** S1 - Welcoming Ceremonies
**Type:** Social / Narrative

## Description

Mayor Kendra Deverin addresses the crowd from the cathedral steps, welcoming visitors and celebrating Sandpoint recovery since the fire. Her speech is brief and warm. Sheriff Hemlock stands nearby, scanning the crowd.

Father Zantus follows with an invocation to Desna - gentle, sincere, unhurried.

## PC Opportunity

- Approaching Mayor Deverin: Diplomacy DC 6 or simply introduce yourself - she is welcoming.
- Approaching Sheriff Hemlock: He acknowledges the PC but keeps scanning the perimeter. He is working, not socialising.
- Approaching Father Zantus: He is occupied with the ceremony; he will speak freely afterward.
