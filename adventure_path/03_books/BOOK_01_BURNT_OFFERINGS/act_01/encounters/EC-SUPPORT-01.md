# EC-SUP-01 - Aid the Wounded

**Scene:** S6 - Aftermath and Brief Social
**Type:** Support / Roleplay
**Trigger:** All goblin threats resolved (attack_repelled event fires)

## Situation

Several festival-goers are injured. Father Zantus is treating the wounded on the cathedral steps. Militia are securing the perimeter and accounting for the dead. Fires are being extinguished.

## PC Options

Helping Father Zantus:
- Heal DC 10: bandage a wound, stabilise an unconscious civilian
- Channel energy or cure spells: restore HP normally

Assessing damage:
- Knowledge (local) DC 12: 2-4 stalls destroyed, 1 building scorched
- Perception DC 10: find a goblin trinket left behind (no mechanical value)

Talking to survivors: Civilians are shaken and grateful. No useful information about goblin origins.

## Milestone: Level 2

PCs reach Level 2 at first natural rest after this scene. Award when the party retires for the night at the Rusty Dragon.
