# EC-INF-01 — Goblin Capture & Interrogation

**Scene:** S4 or S5 — any wave during the goblin assault  
**Type:** Conditional — only if a goblin is captured alive  
**Reward:** +50 XP bonus; Act II investigation hook

---

## Capturing a Goblin

A goblin is reduced to 0 HP by nonlethal damage (e.g. subdual strike, sleep spell, Intimidate to surrender).

Alternatively: a warrior who fails its morale check and cannot flee (cornered) may surrender. Will DC 8 check — on failure it fights to the death anyway.

Warchanter does not surrender.

---

## Interrogation

A captured goblin is frightened and incoherent. It won't answer calm questions.

| Method | DC | Time | Result on Success |
|--------|-----|------|-------------------|
| Intimidate | 15 | 1 min | Goblin talks |
| Diplomacy | 20 | 10 min | Goblin talks (less reliably) |
| Charm Person | auto | 1 round | Goblin answers freely |

### What the Goblin Knows (GM-only)

Give one fact per successful check. Stop after three facts — the goblin doesn't know more.

1. "We come from the woods north! Chief Ripnugget says burn the town!" (directional hint only; no location name)
2. "There's a pretty woman who told us to come. She smells like fire." (Nualia reference — forbidden to name or describe further)
3. "Thistletop is our home! Big fort in the sea-trees!" (only give this if Intimidate exceeds DC 20 or the PC is very persuasive)

**Hard constraint:** Do NOT give Nualia's name, Thistletop's full name (hint only), or any explanation of the attack's purpose.

---

## Disposition

After interrogation the goblin is a prisoner. Sheriff Hemlock takes custody of it. It escapes during the night; this is off-screen and does not require PC involvement. The hint(s) given are now available as investigation leads.
