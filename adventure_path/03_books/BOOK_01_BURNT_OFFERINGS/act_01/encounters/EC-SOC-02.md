# EC-SOC-02 - Festival Games and Food

**Scene:** S2 - Festival Social Phase
**Type:** Social / Exploration

## Description

The festival square is crowded with booths. Food vendors sell meat pies, festival bread, and spiced cider. A few informal games are running: a ring-toss, an arm-wrestling station, and a small stage with a bard performing.

## Activities

Eating and drinking: No roll. Describe sensory details. Minor cost (1-2 cp).

Ring-toss (prize: live swallowtail in a small cage):
- Dexterity DC 12 or Ranged attack roll vs AC 12
- Success: player keeps the butterfly or releases it

Arm-wrestling (Sandpoint labourer, Str 16):
- CMB opposed check (the labourer rolls +3)
- Win: crowd cheers, minor reputation bump
- Lose: the labourer buys the PC a drink

Listening to the bard: Perform (string) result 14. PCs can request songs; the bard knows common Varisian folk tunes.

No stakes. No lasting consequences. Atmosphere building only.
