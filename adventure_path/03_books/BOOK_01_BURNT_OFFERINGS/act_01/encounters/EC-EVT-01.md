# EC-EVT-01 - Release of Swallowtails

**Scene:** S1 - Welcoming Ceremonies
**Type:** Event / Narrative
**No combat. No roll required.**

## Description

Father Zantus concludes the opening prayer and signals festival volunteers. Dozens of wicker cages are opened simultaneously, releasing hundreds of live swallowtail butterflies into the air above the square. The crowd responds with applause and cheers.

The butterflies are sacred to Desna - goddess of travel, dreams, and the stars. Their release is both a symbolic blessing of the new cathedral and a farewell to the old one lost to fire five years ago.

## GM Notes

Describe the visual spectacle. A few butterflies may land on PCs or bystanders. Keep the tone warm and celebratory. Do NOT foreshadow danger during this beat.

## Transition

Marks exit of Scene S1 (Welcoming Ceremonies). Transitions to S2 (Festival Social Phase).
