## Special Abilities

**Allergic Reaction (Ex)**
A goblin dog's dander is highly irritating to all non-goblinoid creatures. Any non-goblinoid creature that:
- takes damage from the goblin dog's bite
- hits a goblin dog with a natural weapon or unarmed strike
- comes into bodily contact with a goblin dog (grapple, riding, etc.)

…must make a **Fort DC 12** save or break out in an itching rash.

**Rash effect:** –2 penalty to Dexterity and Charisma for 1 day. Multiple reactions do not stack. Removed instantly by *remove disease* or any magical healing. This is a disease effect. Save DC is Constitution-based.
